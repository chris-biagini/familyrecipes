# Simple Tomato Sauce

A versatile base sauce. Make a double batch and freeze half for busy weeknights.

Makes: 4 servings

## Cook the sauce.

- Olive oil, 2 tbsp
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Minced.
- Onion, 1: Finely diced.
- Tomato passata, 680 g
- Basil, a few leaves: Torn.
- Salt

Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook garlic and onion until soft, about 5 minutes. Add passata, basil, and a pinch of salt. Simmer uncovered for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened.
