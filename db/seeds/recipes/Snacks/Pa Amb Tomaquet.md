# Pa Amb Tomaquet

Good as an after-school snack.

Category: Snacks
Serves: 1

## Prepare tomatoes.

- Tomatoes (fresh): Can substitute canned.

If using canned tomatoes, pulse in blender to form a coarse puree.

If using fresh tomatoes, slice in half. Then rub open face of each half onto a coarse grater until the flesh is gone. Discard the skin.

## Assemble.

- Bread: Toast.
- Garlic: Optional.
- Olive oil
- Maldon salt

If using garlic, peel and rub on surface of toasted bread.

Top bread with tomatoes, olive oil, and salt.

Based on a recipe from [Jose Andres](https://joseandres.com/).
