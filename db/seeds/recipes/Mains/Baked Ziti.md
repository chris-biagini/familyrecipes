# Baked Ziti

Mom's famous baked pasta.

Category: Mains
Serves: 4

## Cook pasta and preheat oven.

- Mezze rigatoni
- Salt
- Pasta sauce (jarred)

Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add salt. Cook pasta according to package directions. 

Drain pasta, return to pot, and combine with sauce.

Preheat oven to 350ยบ.

## Assemble and bake.

- Mozzarella (low-moisture): Shredded.
- Ricotta
- Parmesan cheese

Spread a portion of the pasta into a 9x13 baking dish. Sprinkle with mozzarella and a bit of parmesan. Add a few dollops of ricotta.

Continue layering remaining pasta and cheese. 

Bake for about 30 minutes.

---

Of course, you can use actual ziti, but (1) rigatoni are better than ziti and (2) "baked mezze rigatoni" sounds ridiculous.
