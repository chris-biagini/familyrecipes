# Honey Rolls

Good for (Impossible) burgers.

## Make Dough.

- Olive oil, 25 g
- Honey, 25 g
- Yeast, 10 g
- Salt, 5 g
- Water, 160 g
- Flour (all-purpose), 250g

Whisk together oil, honey, yeast, salt, and water. Whisk in half the flour, then stir in the rest.

Knead until smooth, then form dough into ball.

Cover and bulk ferment for about 30-60 minutes or until doubled in size.

## Pre-shape rolls and bake.

- Cornmeal
- Sesame seeds: Optional.

Divide the dough into 115-g pieces, then pre-shape into balls.

Allow to rest for 20 minutes, then gently press each roll into a wide disk. Optionally, dust with flour, spray with water, and dip into sesame seeds.

Dust parchment-lined baking sheet with cornmeal, then place rolls a few inches apart.

Cover and let rise for 30-60 minutes while preheating oven to 450Â° convection bake.

Bake for about 15 minutes.
