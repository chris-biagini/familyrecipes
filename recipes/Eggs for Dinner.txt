# Eggs for Dinner

Simple!

## Fry eggs.

- Eggs
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper

Fry eggs. (Or scramble them.)

## Make salad.

- Salad greens
- Salt and pepper
- Oil
- Vinegar

Place greens in large bowl. Season with salt and pepper, then oil, then vinegar, mixing and tasting as you go.

## Plate and serve.

- Bread

Toast bread. Serve eggs on toast with salad on the side.
