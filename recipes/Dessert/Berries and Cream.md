# Berries and Cream

Super-fast summertime dessert.

Category: Dessert

## Prepare berries.

- Berries, 100 g
- Sugar (white), 10 g
- Salt, 0.1 g

Scale ingredients as necessary.

Combine berries, sugar, and salt. Let sit, tossing occassionally, up to two hours.

## Serve.

- Whipped cream

Serve berries with whipped cream.

---

Based on a recipe from ChefSteps.

Great with a slice of pound cake, granola, crumbled cookies, or ice cream.
