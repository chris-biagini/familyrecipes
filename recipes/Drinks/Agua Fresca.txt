# Agua Fresca

A refreshing summer drink.

## Blend all ingredients.

- Watermelon, 100 g
- Water, 100 g
- Honey, 10 g
- Limes

Scale ingredients as needed.

Cut watermelon into chunks. Add to blender with water (some of which can be ice!) and honey. Blend until smooth.

Add lime juice to taste.

---

Based on a recipe from [Rick Bayless](https://www.rickbayless.com/recipe/watermelon-cooler/).
