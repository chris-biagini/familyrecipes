# Roasted Potatoes

## Prep oven and parboil potatoes.

- Potatoes, 800 g: Cut into large pieces.
- Water, 1000 g
- Salt, 10 g
- Baking soda, 2 g

Preheat oven to 450ÂºF with convection. Place a half-sheet pan on the middle rack.

Combine baking soda, salt, and water in a pot and bring to a boil. 

Add potatoes and cook until tender, about 10 minutes.

## Roast potatoes.

- Olive oil
- Salt
- Black pepper

Drain potatoes and let dry for a few seconds in pot. Toss with oil and season lightly. Stir potatoes to rough up surface.

Transfer potatoes to sheet pan. Roast for 20 minutes. Flip over and roast about 20-30 more minutes, or until well-browned.

Season to taste and serve.
