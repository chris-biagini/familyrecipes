# Roasted Broccoli

~

## Prepare broccoli.

- Broccoli: Cut into small florets.
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Black pepper

Toss broccoli, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.

## Roast.

Preheat oven to 400ยบ.

Add broccoli to half-sheet pan. Roast for 20 minutes or until tender and lightly browned.

---

Use the same procedure for Brussels sprouts.
