# Lentil Soup

Basic lentil soup with tomato

## Prepare base.

- Olive oil, 100 g
- Carrot, 1: Grate.
- Onion, 1: Grate.
- Garlic, 2 cloves: Chop coarsely.
- Tomato paste, 30 g

In a deep pot, saut√© onion and carrot in oil. Add garlic and tomato paste and cook until fragrant.

## Add and cook lentils.

- Lentils, 1 lb: Check for stones, then rinse.
- Water, 6 cups
- Vegetable bouillon, 1 Tbsp
- Bay leaf, 1

Add lentils, water, bouillon, and bay leaf. Bring to a boil and continue boiling for 15 minutes.

Reduce heat to a simmer and continue cooking on for 30 minutes or until lentils are soft. Add more water as needed to maintain consistency.

## Finish and serve.

- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon

Season to taste with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
