# Lentil Soup

Basic lentil soup with tomato.

## Prepare base.

- Olive oil, 50 g
- Onion, 1: Chop finely.
- Carrot, 1: Chop finely.
- Tomato paste, 30 g

In a deep pot, saut√© onion and carrot in oil. Add tomato paste and cook until fragrant.

## Add and cook lentils.

- Lentils, 225 g: Check for stones, then rinse.
- Water, 6 cups
- Vegetable bouillon, 1 small spoonful

Add lentils, water, and bouillon to pot. Bring to a gentle boil.

Cook for 20 minutes, or until lentils have softened but are not quite done.

## Add pasta.

- Pasta, 150 g: Ditalini or similar.
- Water

Add the pasta and additional water, if necessary to maintain consistency.

Continue cooking until pasta is done, about 15 minutes.

## Finish and serve.

- Salt
- Black pepper

Season to taste with salt and pepper.
