Lebanese pilaf with lentils and caramelized onions.

## Caramelize onions.

- Olive oil, 50 g
- Onions, 450 g: Slice thinly, pole-to-pole.
- Salt
- Pepper

Place olive oil and onions in a large saucepan over medium heat. Season to taste.

Cook gently, stirring frequently, for 15-20 minutes or until the onions are a deep golden brown.

## Start the lentils.

- Lentils, 225 g: Check for debris and rinse.
- Water, 1000 g
- Kombu, 1 small piece: Optional.

Add the lentils to the saucepan and stir, scraping up any onion bits from the pan.

Add water and kombu, if using, and bring to a boil.

Cook for 10 minutes, maintaining a low boil. Remove kombu.

## Add the rice and finish cooking.

- Jasmine rice, 225: Rinse.
- Salt

Add rice, stir, and return to a low boil. Taste liquid for seasoning and add more salt if necessary. Add water if pan looks too dry.

Cover pan and cook over very low heat for 15 minutes.

Check rice for doneness. Add water, if needed. Cook a bit longer if rice is not quite done, removing lid if dish is too wet.

## Finish and serve.

- Lemon
- Salt
- Pepper

Correct for acid and seasoning. Serve.
