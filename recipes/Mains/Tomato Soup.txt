# Tomato Soup

Rich tomato soup with olive oil.

## Saute aromatics.

- Olive oil, 100 g
- Onion, 150 g: Roughly chop.
- Garlic, 2 cloves: Roughly chop.

In large saucepan, saute onion and garlic in oil.

Transfer everything to blender.

## Blend soup in batches.

- Bread, 60 g: Tear into small pieces.
- Tomatoes (canned), 1600 g: Two 28-ounce cans.
- Vegetable bouillon, 5 g
- Water, 500 g

To blender, add bread, one can of tomatoes, and vegetable bouillon. Puree until smooth. Return to saucepan.

Add second can of tomatoes to blender, and puree until smooth. Transfer to saucepan.

Add water to blender, pulse a few times, and transfer to saucepan.

## Finish and serve.

- Salt
- Black pepper

Bring soup to a gentle simmer. Season to taste.
