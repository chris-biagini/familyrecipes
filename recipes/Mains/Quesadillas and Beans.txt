## Make the quesadillas.

- Tortillas (large), 4
- Monterey jack cheese
- Olive oil

Heat a large griddle to about 350ÂºF. Working in batches, make quesadillas, adding a bit of oil to the griddle before laying each tortilla.

## Make the beans.

- Black beans (canned), 2 cans
- Olive oil
- Tomato salsa
- Salt

Drain some, but not all, of the bean cooking liquid. Add to small pot and bring to simmer. Add salsa, oil, and seasonings to taste.

## Serve.

- Greek yogurt
- Hot sauce

Slice quesadillas and serve with beans, greek yogurt, and hot sauce.

This is not haute cuisine by any stretch, but it's fast and the kids eat it :)
