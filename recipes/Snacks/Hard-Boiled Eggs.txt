# Hard-Boiled Eggs

Protein!

## Make ice bath.

- Water
- Ice

Make ice bath in large bowl.

## Cook eggs.

- Eggs

Fill steamer pot with water and bring to a boil.

Add eggs to steamer basket and cover.

Steam 6 minutes for soft boiled eggs, 12 minutes for hard boiled eggs.

Based on a recipe from [Serious Eats](https://www.seriouseats.com/steamed-hard-boiled-eggs-recipe).
