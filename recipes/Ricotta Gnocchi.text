# Ricotta Gnocchi

Cheese dumplings in tomato sauce

## Prepare sauce.

- Olive oil, 100 g
- Onion, 50 g: Peel and finely chop.
- Carrot, 50 g: Peel and finely chop.
- Tomato, 500 g: Crush or puree.
- Tomato paste, 50 g
- Salt and pepper

In a large heavy-bottomed pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and carrot, season lightly, and cook gently, stirring until vegetables are soft and just barely golden. Stir in the tomato puree and set to simmer while preparing the gnocchi.

## Prepare gnocchi.

- Ricotta, 425 g
- Parmesan, 75 g
- Egg, 1
- Salt, 5 g
- Flour, 150 g: Plus extra for dusting.

Mix together Parmesan, egg, and salt. Stir in ricotta, then flour. Mix thoroughly. Dust dough and cutting board with flour, then turn dough out onto board.

Cut dough into manageable pieces, roll into thin logs, and cut into segments.

## Poach gnocchi and combine with sauce.

Fill a pot with water and bring to a boil over high heat. Cook gnocchi until nearly doubled in size, about 2-3 minutes, then use a slotted spoon to remove from water and add to sauce.
