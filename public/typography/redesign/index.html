Redesign Explorations

Riffs on Option F — the Woven baseline.

  • V1: Open Air
    Drop the content card. Full-width with generous margins and breathing room. A lighter, more editorial feel with the linen texture carrying the whole page.
  • V2: Warm Earth
    Deeper, earthier palette with terracotta and sage tones. A side-anchored nav. Designed for warmth and comfort — like a well-loved cookbook.
  • V3: Vibrant Thread
    Brings back the favicon's bold red as a first-class accent. Richer color contrast, a compact sticky nav, and crisp visual rhythm that pairs with the gingham icon.
  • V4: Quiet Luxury
    Maximum restraint. Whisper-thin rules, generous whitespace, a nearly monochrome palette with one precise red accent. The recipe as a beautiful document.

V4 Riffs — Adding Brand

  • V4a: Crossing Threads
    Two semi-transparent red stripes — one horizontal under the nav, one vertical along the content edge — cross at the top-left, creating a tiny gingham moment. Section breaks use a miniature check square.
  • V4b: Gingham Mark
    The favicon rendered in pure CSS becomes the logo mark in the nav and section dividers. A large, ghosted check watermarks behind each page title. The favicon is the brand.
  • V4c: Twin Rules
    Every rule becomes a pair of thin lines with a small gap — the two bars of the gingham abstracted to pure geometry. Twin red stripes at the top, twin gray rules under headers, twin dividers everywhere. The doubled line is the brand motif.
