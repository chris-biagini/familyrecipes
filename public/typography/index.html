Typography Options

Each page below shows the same recipe content with a different typography treatment. Open them in separate tabs to flip between them, or compare against the live site.

Option A: Futura Refined
Keep Futura everywhere, but drop the ALL CAPS on recipe step headers. Title case + tighter letter-spacing lets the long step names breathe. Category headers stay uppercase since they're short labels.
Changes: text-transform, letter-spacing, font-weight on h2s
Option B: Futura + Lora
Keep Futura for UI/labels, but swap Source Sans for Lora (a warm text serif) as the body font. The geometric-sans / warm-serif contrast is a classic cookbook pairing and sits well with the gingham.
Changes: body font to Lora, adjusted sizing
Option C: Fraunces + Source Sans
Replace Futura headers with Fraunces, a variable "soft serif" with personality. Warm, slightly quirky, very food-appropriate. Source Sans stays for body text. No ALL CAPS anywhere.
Changes: display font to Fraunces, drop uppercase treatment
Option D: Futura + Bitter
Keep Futura for short UI labels (nav, categories) but use Bitter, a sturdy slab serif, for recipe step headers and body. Slab serifs have the geometric DNA that harmonizes with Futura but add warmth.
Changes: recipe headers and body to Bitter slab serif
Option E: Refined Heritage
Full redesign. Cormorant Garamond (elegant Italian-cookbook serif) for headings, DM Sans for body. Gingham ghosted to ~10% opacity — a watermark, not a tablecloth. Deeper burgundy palette. Same warmth, more sophistication.
Redesign: fonts, colors, gingham opacity, spacing, hierarchy
Option F: Woven
Gingham abstracted into a vibe. Background becomes a faint diagonal crosshatch of thin lines. Red shows up as a "thread" accent on borders and dividers. Instrument Serif (modern, graceful) for display, Outfit for body. Double-border "stitched" card edges.
Redesign: abstract gingham, new fonts, thread-accent system, card borders
Option G: Night Kitchen
No rules. Dark, warm, dramatic — like cooking late at night with the kitchen lit and everything else dark. Bodoni Moda (ultra high-contrast display serif) at huge sizes, Libre Franklin body. Charcoal with amber/copper accents. No gingham at all.
Redesign: dark theme, entirely new aesthetic, no gingham
