Beef Stroganoff Recipe

A classic Russian dish featuring tender strips of beef in a rich, creamy
mushroom sauce served over egg noodles. This version uses sour cream and
Worcestershire sauce for depth of flavor.

The recipe calls for beef sirloin, mushrooms, onion, garlic, beef broth,
sour cream, Worcestershire sauce, and egg noodles. Season with salt,
pepper, and paprika.

Preparation takes about 30 minutes. Serves 4-6.

Source: adapted from Julia Child
