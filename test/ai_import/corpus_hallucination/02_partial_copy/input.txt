The BEST Chicken Parmesan
★★★★★ 4.9 (832 reviews)

Jump to Recipe | Print Recipe

You guys, this chicken parm is EVERYTHING. I've been making it for years and
it never gets old. The secret is pounding the chicken thin and using freshly
grated parmesan in the breading.

INGREDIENTS

For the chicken:
- Boneless skinless chicken breasts
- Flour for dredging
- Eggs
- Italian breadcrumbs
- Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
- Salt and pepper
- Olive oil for frying

For the sauce and topping:
- Marinara sauce
- Mozzarella cheese, shredded
- Fresh basil
