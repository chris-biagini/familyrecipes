# Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe - RecipeTin Eats

## Overview
This page presents a recipe for what the author describes as "my best grilled cheese sandwich," published on July 28, 2023, and last modified in May 2025. The post is categorized under "Sandwiches and Sliders."

## Key Recipe Components

**Ingredients:**
- Two thick-cut sourdough bread slices (1.3-1.5cm)
- Salted butter for spreading
- Vintage cheddar or gruyere cheese, freshly grated
- Fresh mozzarella, grated

**Cooking Method:**
The recipe emphasizes stovetop preparation using a heavy-based skillet. The author notes that "butter both sides" of the bread and describes a light toasting step before adding cheese. The technique involves cooking approximately 3 minutes per side to achieve a crispy exterior while keeping the cheese melted inside.

## Notable Details

The author states the approach differs from typical sandwich makers, suggesting stovetop cooking produces superior results. The recipe yields one serving and takes approximately 12 minutes total (5 minutes prep, 7 minutes cooking).
