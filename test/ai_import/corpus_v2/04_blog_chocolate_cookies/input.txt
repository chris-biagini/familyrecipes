# Best Chocolate Chip Cookies (Popular Recipe!) - Sally's Baking Addiction

## Recipe Overview

This webpage presents a popular chocolate chip cookie recipe that emphasizes achieving an ultra-soft, chewy texture. The recipe uses melted butter, more brown sugar than white sugar, cornstarch, and an extra egg yolk as key ingredients for optimal chewiness.

## Key Ingredients

The recipe calls for:
- All-purpose flour (spooned & leveled)
- Baking soda and cornstarch
- Unsalted butter (melted and cooled)
- Both light/dark brown sugar and granulated sugar
- One large egg plus an additional egg yolk
- Vanilla extract
- Semi-sweet chocolate chips or chunks

## Preparation Instructions

The dough requires refrigeration for at least 2 hours before baking, with overnight chilling recommended to prevent overspreading. Bakers should scoop approximately 2-3 tablespoons of dough per cookie, shaping them cylindrically rather than flat to encourage thicker baking results.

## Baking Details

Bake at 325°F for 13-14 minutes. "The centers will look very soft, but the cookies will continue to set as they cool."
