## Recipe Information

**Title:** Roast Chicken

**Author:** Nagi | RecipeTin Eats

**Description:** A roast chicken "slathered with a garlic-herb-lemon butter then oven roasted to golden crispy perfection. Juicy on the inside" with "liquid gold pan juices loaded with flavour."

**Yield:** 5 servings

**Prep Time:** 15 minutes

**Cook Time:** 90 minutes

**Total Time:** 105 minutes

## Key Ingredients
- 1.75-2 kg whole chicken
- Salt and pepper
- Olive oil
- Lemon, rosemary, butter
- Garlic, sage, parsley
- Dry white wine or chicken broth
- Onion and garlic bulb

## Basic Cooking Method
The recipe involves preparing a garlic-herb-lemon butter, loosening the chicken skin, distributing the butter mixture underneath, and roasting at 220°C initially, then reducing to 180°C until internal temperature reaches 75°C.
