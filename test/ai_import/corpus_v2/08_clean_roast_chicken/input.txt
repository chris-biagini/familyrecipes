# Roast Chicken Recipe - RecipeTin Eats

## Recipe Overview
This is a garlic-herb-lemon butter roasted chicken recipe from RecipeTin Eats by Nagi. The dish yields 5 servings with a prep time of 15 minutes and cook time of 90 minutes (total 105 minutes).

## Ingredients

**For the Chicken:**
- 1.75-2 kg (3.5-4 lb) whole chicken, patted dry
- Salt and pepper
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 1 lemon, quartered
- 3 rosemary sprigs

**Garlic-Herb-Lemon Butter:**
- 100 g (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp sage, finely chopped
- 2 tsp rosemary, finely chopped
- 1 tbsp parsley, finely chopped
- 1/2 tsp each salt and black pepper

**Pan Ingredients:**
- 1 cup (250 ml) dry white wine or low sodium chicken broth
- 1 onion, quartered
- 1 garlic bulb, halved horizontally

## Instructions

1. Remove chicken from fridge 30 minutes before cooking
2. Preheat oven to 220 C/450 F (standard) or 200 C/430 F (fan/convection), placing shelf in middle
3. Mix butter ingredients together, adding juice from 2 lemon wedges
4. Place chicken in roasting pan and loosen skin using a spoon on the breast and drumsticks
5. Prop chicken upright and drizzle herb butter under skin, reserving some for the surface
6. Drizzle remaining butter over the chicken and squeeze lemon juice over it
7. Stuff used lemon wedges and rosemary inside the chicken cavity
8. Tie drumstick ends with string and tuck wing tips underneath
9. Sprinkle salt and pepper over entire surface
10. Place onion and garlic in pan, set chicken on top, pour wine around it, and drizzle with oil
11. Roast for 10 minutes at high temperature, then reduce to 180 C/350 F for 1 hour 15 minutes, basting twice (at 30 min and 1 hr). Check that internal temperature reaches 75 C/165 F
12. Rest for 15 minutes without covering (to maintain crispy skin)
13. Serve with pan juices; discard onion but use roasted garlic

## Nutrition
Per serving: 505 kcal (based on 399g serving size)
