## Recipe Description
"Made-from-scratch Chicken Tikka Masala recipe with the signature yoghurt marinated chargrilled chicken smothered in an incredible spice infused curry sauce."

The dish features low spice level with 3-24 hours marinating time required.

## Ingredients

**Chicken Tikka Marinade:**
- 600g boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cubed
- ½ cup plain yogurt
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp each salt, cumin, ground coriander, paprika
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- ⅛ tsp cayenne pepper
- Pinch black pepper
- 2 tsp lemon juice

**Curry Sauce Spices:**
- 2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tbsp garam masala
- 2 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp cumin
- ⅛ tsp cardamom powder
- ⅛ tsp cayenne pepper

**Sauce Base:**
- 3 tbsp vegetable oil
- 30g unsalted butter or ghee
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
- 6 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1⅔ cups tomato passata
- 1⅔ cups water
- 100ml cream
- 1 tsp sugar
- 50g unsalted butter or ghee
- Basmati rice for serving

## Instructions

**Chicken Tikka:**
1. Combine marinade ingredients and coat chicken pieces
2. Cover and refrigerate minimum 3 hours (overnight preferred)
3. Heat oil in non-stick pan over high heat until smoking
4. Sear chicken in batches until charred, 2 minutes per side
5. Remove and set aside

**Sauce:**
1. Wipe pan clean, reduce heat to medium-high
2. Add oil and butter; sauté onions, ginger, and salt until golden (5-7 minutes)
3. Add garlic and paprika; cook 2 minutes
4. Add spices; cook 2 minutes, stirring
5. Pour in tomato passata and water; bring to simmer
6. Cover and simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally
7. Blend sauce with stick blender until smooth
8. Return to pan; add cream, sugar, and butter
9. Add chicken and simmer until cooked through
10. Serve over basmati rice with optional cilantro
