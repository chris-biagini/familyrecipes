# Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe - RecipeTin Eats

## Recipe Overview
This is a quick weeknight bolognese that can be prepared in approximately 40 minutes total (10 minutes prep, 30 minutes cooking), though a slower version requiring 2-2.5 hours is also available for deeper flavor development.

## Key Ingredients
The recipe calls for ground beef, red wine, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, beef bouillon cubes, Worcestershire sauce, and bay leaves. The creator notes three special components that add depth: "Worcestershire Sauce, Beef Bouillon Cubes and a touch of sugar."

## Cooking Method
The sauce is built by sautéing aromatics, browning meat, reducing wine, then simmering all components together. The recipe yields approximately 5 servings.
