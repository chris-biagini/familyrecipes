# Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe - RecipeTin Eats

## Recipe Overview
This is a quick 30-minute Spaghetti Bolognese with depth of flavor, authored by Nagi from RecipeTin Eats. The recipe yields 5 servings with a total time of 40 minutes (10 minutes prep, 30 minutes cook).

## Ingredients

**For the Bolognese sauce:**
- 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 onion, finely chopped (brown, yellow or white)
- 1 lb / 500g beef mince (ground beef) [OR half pork, half beef]
- 1/2 cup (125 ml) dry red wine [substitute: water or beef broth/stock]
- 2 beef bouillon cubes, crumbled or granulated
- 800g / 28 oz can crushed tomato [or tomato passata]
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 tsp white sugar (if needed)
- 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 dried bay leaves
- 2 sprigs fresh thyme [or 1/2 tsp dried thyme or oregano]
- 3/4 tsp cooking salt (kosher salt)
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 400 g / 13 oz spaghetti, dried
- Parmesan cheese and finely chopped parsley (optional garnish)

## Instructions

**Step 1 - Saute:**
Heat oil in a large pot or deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and garlic; cook 3 minutes until light golden and softened.

**Step 2 - Cook beef:**
Turn heat to high and add beef. Cook, breaking it up as you go, until browned.

**Step 3 - Reduce wine:**
Add red wine. Bring to simmer and cook 1 minute, scraping the bottom of the pot, until the alcohol smell is gone.

**Step 4 - Simmer:**
Add remaining ingredients. Stir, bring to a simmer then reduce heat to medium so it bubbles gently. Cook 20-30 minutes (no lid), adding water if sauce gets too thick. Stir occasionally.

**Alternative - Slow simmer option:**
Add 3/4 cup water, cover with lid and simmer on very low for 2-2.5 hours, stirring every 30 minutes. Uncover and simmer 20 minutes to thicken sauce.

**Step 5 - Season and serve:**
Taste and add more salt if desired. Serve over spaghetti or proceed with tossing method below.

**Optional Tossing Method:**

1. Bring large pot of salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook per packet directions minus 1 minute.

2. Scoop out a mug of pasta cooking water and set aside, then drain pasta.

3. Add pasta into bolognese sauce with about 1/2 cup (125 ml) reserved pasta water over medium heat. Toss gently 1 1/2-2 minutes until spaghetti turns red and sauce thickens.

4. Divide between bowls. Garnish with parmesan and parsley if desired.
