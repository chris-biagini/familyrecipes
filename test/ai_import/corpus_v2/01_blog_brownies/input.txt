# Flourless Chocolate Brownies (Gluten Free) - RecipeTin Eats

## Recipe Overview

This is a flourless chocolate brownie recipe from RecipeTin Eats, authored by Nagi. The recipe yields 16 pieces and requires about 40 minutes total (15 minutes prep, 25 minutes baking).

## Key Ingredients

The recipe calls for:
- 1 cup dark chocolate chips or melts
- 85g unsalted butter
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 2/3 cup almond meal
- 1/3 cup rice flour
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/4 tsp salt

## Basic Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (325°F) with rack in lower third
2. Grease and line a 20cm square pan
3. Melt chocolate and butter together (microwave in 30-second intervals)
4. Cool 5 minutes, then add vanilla and sugar
5. Mix in eggs, then almond meal and rice flour
6. Bake 25 minutes until slightly puffed with moist center
7. Cool 30+ minutes before cutting into squares
