# No-Knead Crusty White Bread Recipe

This King Arthur Baking recipe creates artisan-style bread through an overnight refrigeration method requiring minimal active work.

## Key Details

**Ingredients:**
- 7½ cups (900g) all-purpose flour
- 3 cups (680g) lukewarm water
- 1 tablespoon (18g) table salt
- 1½ tablespoons (14g) instant or active dry yeast

**Timing:**
- Prep: 5 minutes
- Cook: 40 minutes
- Total: approximately 5 hours 35 minutes

**Yield:** 12 slices per loaf; produces 3-4 loaves total

## Process Overview

The method involves mixing ingredients into a sticky dough, allowing it to rise at room temperature for two hours, then refrigerating for minimum 2-7 days. When ready to bake, tear off approximately one-quarter of the dough, shape it, let it rise about 60 minutes, score the surface, and bake at 450°F for 25-35 minutes with steam.
