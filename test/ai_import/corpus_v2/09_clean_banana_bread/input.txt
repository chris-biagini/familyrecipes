# Banana Bread Recipe - Love and Lemons

## Recipe Overview

This is a straightforward banana bread recipe from Love and Lemons, created by Jeanine Donofrio and Phoebe Moore. The recipe description notes: "This easy banana bread recipe is the BEST way to use ripe bananas! It's super moist, delicious, and packed with banana flavor."

## Ingredients

- 2 cups mashed very ripe banana (about 4 large)
- ½ cup cane sugar or brown sugar
- ½ cup melted butter or vegetable oil (plus more for the pan)
- 2 large eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1½ cups all-purpose flour (spooned and leveled)
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon sea salt
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
- ½ cup chopped walnuts (plus 2 tablespoons for topping)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F and grease an 8x4 or 9x5-inch loaf pan
2. Whisk together mashed banana, sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl
3. In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg
4. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, stirring until just combined without overmixing; fold in ½ cup walnuts
5. Pour batter into prepared pan and top with remaining 2 tablespoons walnuts
6. Bake 50-60 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean and top springs back when touched

**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 55 minutes
**Total Time:** 70 minutes
**Yield:** 8 servings
