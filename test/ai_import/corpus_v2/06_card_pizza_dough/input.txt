# Pizza Dough Recipe - RecipeTin Eats

## Page Content Summary

This is a recipe page for homemade pizza dough from RecipeTin Eats, authored by Nagi. The page includes comprehensive instructions for making Italian-style pizza crust.

## Key Recipe Information

**Yield:** 3 pizzas (30cm/12")

**Time:** 30 minutes prep + 30 minutes cook

**Main Ingredients:**
- 600g bread or pizza flour
- 2 tsp instant yeast
- 2½ tsp salt
- 4 tsp sugar
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 330ml warm water

## Instructions Overview

The recipe offers two methods: hand-kneading (5 minutes) or food processor (40 seconds). The process includes:

1. Mix dry ingredients, then add wet components
2. First rise: 1-2 hours until doubled
3. Optional: Refrigerate up to 5 days for flavor development
4. Form three dough balls for second rise (1 hour)
5. Stretch into 30cm bases
6. Top with sauce, cheese, and preferred toppings
7. Bake at 275°C for 10 minutes
