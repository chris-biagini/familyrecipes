## Main Content

**Title:** Chicken Pot Pie

**Author:** Nagi | **Published:** October 22, 2018 | **Updated:** May 11, 2025

**Description:** A creamy chicken and vegetable filling with thyme, topped with flaky puff pastry. The recipe makes 4 large, 6 medium, or 1 large pie and freezes well.

### Recipe Stats
- **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 60 minutes
- **Total Time:** 75 minutes
- **Servings:** 4-6 people
- **Rating:** 4.93/5 stars (170 ratings, 17 reviews)

### Ingredients
- 600g chicken breast or boneless thighs
- 2 cups milk
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 2 tsp chicken/vegetable stock powder
- 2 sprigs thyme (optional)
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 large carrots, chopped
- 3 celery ribs, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 50g butter
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1/3 cup white wine
- 1/3 cup flour
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 cup frozen peas
- 2 sheets puff pastry
- 1 egg, whisked

### Instructions

**Chicken:**
1. Place milk, broth, and stock powder in large saucepan; bring to gentle simmer on medium heat. Add chicken and thyme sprigs.
2. Cover and simmer gently on medium-low for 15 minutes (avoid boiling to prevent milk separation).
3. Remove chicken, shred or dice (some uncooked parts are fine). Set poaching liquid aside.

**Chicken Pot Pie:**
1. Melt butter in large pot over medium-high heat. Add garlic and onion; cook 2 minutes. Add thyme, carrot, and celery; cook 3 minutes until softened.
2. Add wine, stir, scraping pot bottom; cook 1.5 minutes until mostly evaporated.
3. Add flour and stir for 1 minute.
4. Add half the reserved poaching liquid, stirring until flour incorporates into thick sludge.
5. Add remaining milk broth, parmesan, and pepper. Stir.
6. Add chicken and peas; stir.
7. Once heated (steam visible), cook 3 minutes, stirring regularly to thicken.
8. Remove from heat. Spoon into oven-proof pots (4 large or 6 small). Cool in fridge at least 30 minutes.

**Assembly & Baking:**
1. Partially thaw puff pastry. Cut rounds 2.5cm wider than pots using a bowl as guide.
2. Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F.
3. Brush pot edges with egg. Top with pastry, folding down edges.
4. Brush pastry with egg. Cut 2cm slit in center with small knife.
5. Bake 35-40 minutes until deep golden.
6. Serve immediately.
