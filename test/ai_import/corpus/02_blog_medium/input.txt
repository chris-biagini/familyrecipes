This chicken stir-fry has been in heavy rotation at our house for years now. It comes together in about 30 minutes, and my kids actually eat it without complaining — which, if you have kids, you know is basically a miracle. The trick is a good marinade and a screaming hot pan. Let's get into it.

Easy Weeknight Chicken Stir-Fry

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 12 minutes
Total Time: 27 minutes
Servings: 4
Course: Main Course

FOR THE MARINADE:
- 1½ lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce (I love this brand!)
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch

FOR THE STIR-FRY:
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced into thin strips
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 2 medium carrots, sliced on the bias
- 3 green onions, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced

FOR THE SAUCE:
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce (I love this brand!)
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 teaspoon sriracha (more or less to taste)

Instructions:

1. In a medium bowl, combine the chicken pieces with the soy sauce, rice vinegar, and cornstarch. Toss to coat evenly and let sit for at least 10 minutes while you prep the vegetables.

2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat until it just begins to smoke. Add the chicken in a single layer and cook without stirring for 2-3 minutes until golden on the bottom. Stir and cook another 2 minutes until cooked through. Transfer chicken to a plate.

3. Add the remaining tablespoon of oil to the wok. Add the bell pepper, broccoli, and carrots. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp. Add the green onions, garlic, and ginger and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.

4. Whisk together the sauce ingredients in a small bowl. Return the chicken to the wok and pour the sauce over everything. Toss to coat and cook for 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly and everything is well coated.

5. Serve immediately over steamed rice.
