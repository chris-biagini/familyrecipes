Recipe: Classic Vinaigrette

A go-to dressing for any salad. Adjust the ratio to taste.

Ingredients:
- 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
- 1 small clove garlic, finely minced
- Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Whisk together the red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, garlic, salt, and pepper in a small bowl until combined.
2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking constantly until the dressing is emulsified and slightly thickened.
3. Taste and adjust seasoning with more salt, pepper, or a tiny splash of vinegar if needed. Use immediately or refrigerate in a jar for up to one week. Shake well before each use.
