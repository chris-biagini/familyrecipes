# Cubano

Inspired by the movie *Chef*.

Serves: 6
Category: Mains

## Advance prep: roast the pork.

- Orange juice, 3 tbsp
- Lime juice, 3 tbsp
- Garlic, 4 tsp: Minced.
- Mirin, 2 tsp
- Cumin, 1 tsp
- Oregano (dried), 1 tsp
- Coriander, 1 tsp
- Salt, 1/2 tsp
- Black pepper, 1/2 tsp
- Pork loin (boneless), 1 lb

Combine all marinade ingredients in a gallon zip-top bag. Add pork, seal, and turn to coat. Marinate in refrigerator at least 2 hours or up to 24 hours.

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Remove pork from marinade; place on rack in roasting pan. Roast 1-1 1/2 hours until an instant-read thermometer reads 145°F (62°C). Rest 15 minutes under foil, then cover tightly and chill overnight for easier slicing. Slice very thin.

## Cook meats; assemble and press.

- Ham (smoked, deli), 18 slices (18 oz)
- Cuban bread, 3 loaves (8 oz each): Split; cut each in half crosswise.
- Swiss cheese, 12 slices
- Pickles (dill), 12 slices: Very thin, lengthwise.
- Mustard (yellow), 1/2 cup
- Butter, 12 tbsp: Melted.

Heat a cast-iron skillet or griddle over medium-high until very hot. Cook pork and ham in batches, turning halfway, until warmed and slightly crispy, about 2 minutes. Keep warm.

Toast bread cut-side down in hot skillet, about 1 minute. Layer pork and ham on bottom halves; top each with 2 cheese slices and 2 pickle slices. Spread mustard generously on top halves; close sandwiches.

Brush skillet with 2 tbsp butter. Add 2 sandwiches bottom-side down; brush tops with 2 tbsp butter. Weight with a heavy skillet, pressing slightly. Toast, turning halfway, until bread is golden and cheese is melted, 6-8 minutes. Repeat in batches with remaining sandwiches and butter.

---

Substitute pulled pork for the roasted pork.

Based on a recipe from Jaelyn Luong on Allrecipes.
