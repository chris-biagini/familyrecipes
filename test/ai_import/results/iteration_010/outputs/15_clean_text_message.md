# Guacamole

Chunky, not smooth.

Category: Sides

- Avocados, 2
- Lime, 1/2
- Salt
- Cilantro: Optional. Finely chopped.
- Jalapeño, 1/2: Minced.

Mash avocados with a fork, keeping it chunky — don't overwork. Squeeze in lime, fold in cilantro and jalapeño, season with salt. Taste and adjust.

---

Use less jalapeño for milder heat.
