# Classic Meatloaf

Just like grandma's.

Category: Mains
Tags: easy, comfort-food

## Mix and shape meatloaf.

- Egg, 1
- Milk, 1/3 cup
- Worcestershire sauce, 1 tsp
- Breadcrumbs (plain), 1/2 cup
- Italian seasoning, 1 tsp
- Garlic powder, 1/2 tsp
- Salt, 1 tsp
- Black pepper, 1/4 tsp
- Onion, 1/2 cup: Minced.
- Ground beef (80/20), 1 1/2 lbs

Preheat oven to 350°F. Whisk egg, milk, and Worcestershire together. In a separate bowl, combine breadcrumbs, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Add beef, egg mixture, breadcrumb mixture, and onion to a large bowl; mix by hand until just combined — don't overmix. Shape into a loaf about 4 inches wide, 8 inches long, and 2 inches tall on a rimmed baking sheet.

## Glaze and bake.

- Ketchup, 1/2 cup
- Sugar (brown), 1 tbsp
- Worcestershire sauce, 1 tbsp
- Mustard (yellow), 1 tsp

Stir glaze ingredients together. Spread evenly over top and sides of loaf. Bake 50-55 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 165°F. Rest 5-10 minutes before slicing.

---

80/20 ground beef gives flavor and keeps the loaf from drying out. Brown sugar can be reduced to 1/2 tbsp if preferred.

Based on a recipe from Beth Moncel.
