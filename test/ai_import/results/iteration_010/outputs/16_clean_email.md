# Chicken Soup

Really easy.

Category: Mains

## Simmer the chicken.

- Chicken (whole), about 3-4 lbs
- Water, about 10-12 cups
- Onion, 1: Quartered.
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Smashed.

Put chicken in pot with water, onion, and garlic. Bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer, skim foam. Cook about 1.5 hours until chicken falls apart.

## Shred chicken; add vegetables.

- Carrots, 2: Sliced.
- Celery, 2 stalks: Sliced.

Remove chicken; cool slightly. Shred meat and discard skin and bones. Add carrots and celery to broth; simmer about 15 minutes until tender. Return shredded chicken to pot.

## Finish and serve.

- Egg noodles, about half a bag
- Dill (fresh), a few sprigs
- Salt
- Black pepper

Cook noodles separately. Season soup with salt, pepper, and dill. Add noodles to each bowl when serving — they go mushy in the pot.

---

Straining the broth is optional. A squeeze of lemon at the table is optional.
