# Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ultra-soft, chewy chocolate chip cookies.

Makes: 24 cookies

- Flour (all-purpose), 2 1/4 cups: Spooned and leveled.
- Baking soda, 1 tsp
- Cornstarch, 1 tbsp
- Butter (unsalted), 1 cup: Melted and cooled.
- Sugar (brown), 3/4 cup
- Sugar (light brown), 3/4 cup
- Sugar (white), 1/4 cup
- Egg, 1 large
- Egg yolk, 1 large
- Vanilla extract, 2 tsp
- Salt
- Chocolate chips (semi-sweet), 2 cups

Whisk together flour, baking soda, cornstarch, and salt in a small bowl. Set aside.

In a large bowl, combine melted butter, brown sugar, light brown sugar, and white sugar. Mix until combined. Add egg, egg yolk, and vanilla extract, and mix until combined. Add flour mixture and stir until just combined. Fold in chocolate chips.

Scoop dough into 2-3 tablespoon portions and shape into cylinders. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight.

Preheat oven to 325°F. Place cookies on baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Bake for 13-14 minutes. The centers will look very soft, but the cookies will continue to set as they cool. Allow to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

---

Overnight chilling is recommended to prevent overspreading. For best results, spoon flour into measuring cup and level with a knife rather than scooping directly from the bag.
