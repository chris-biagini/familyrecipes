# Spaghetti Bolognese

A quick weeknight bolognese ready in 40 minutes.

Serves: 5

## Brown the meat and aromatics.

- Olive oil, 1 tbsp
- Onion, 1: Finely diced.
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Minced.
- Ground beef, 1 lb

Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add onion and garlic, and sauté until softened, about 2-3 minutes. Add ground beef and cook, breaking it up as it cooks, until browned, about 5-7 minutes.

## Build the sauce.

- Red wine, 1/2 cup
- Tomatoes (crushed), 28 oz can
- Tomato paste, 2 tbsp
- Beef bouillon cubes, 2
- Worcestershire sauce, 1 tbsp
- Bay leaves, 2
- Sugar, 1 tsp
- Salt
- Black pepper

Pour in red wine and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan. Simmer until reduced by half, about 2-3 minutes. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, beef bouillon cubes, Worcestershire sauce, bay leaves, and sugar. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Reduce heat to low and simmer for 25-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and flavors meld. Remove bay leaves before serving.

---

For deeper flavor development, simmer the sauce for 2-2.5 hours over low heat instead of 25-30 minutes. Recipe from RecipeTin Eats.
