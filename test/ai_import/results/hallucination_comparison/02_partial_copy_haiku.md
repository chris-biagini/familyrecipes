# Chicken Parmesan

## Prepare chicken.

- Chicken breasts (boneless, skinless)
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Flour
- Eggs
- Italian breadcrumbs
- Parmesan cheese, freshly grated

Pound the chicken breasts thin. Season with salt and pepper. Set up three shallow bowls: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with a mixture of Italian breadcrumbs and freshly grated parmesan cheese. Dredge each chicken breast in flour, shaking off excess, then dip in egg, then coat thoroughly in the breadcrumb mixture.

## Cook and assemble.

- Olive oil
- Marinara sauce
- Mozzarella cheese, shredded
- Fresh basil

Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the breaded chicken until golden brown on both sides. Transfer to a baking dish and top each piece with marinara sauce and shredded mozzarella cheese. Bake at 400°F until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.
