# Homemade Pasta

Makes: 1 lb

- Flour (all-purpose), 2 cups
- Eggs, 3 large
- Olive oil, 1 tablespoon
- Salt, 1/2 teaspoon

Mound the flour on a clean work surface and create a well in the center. Crack the eggs into the well and add the olive oil and salt. Using a fork, beat the eggs together, then gradually incorporate the flour from the sides of the mound until a shaggy dough forms.

Knead the dough by hand for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic. If the dough is too dry, wet your hands slightly; if too wet, dust with a little flour. Wrap in plastic wrap and let rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.

Divide the dough into manageable portions. Using a pasta machine or rolling pin, roll each portion to your desired thickness. Cut into your preferred shape (fettuccine, pappardelle, lasagna sheets, etc.) and lay on a floured surface to dry for at least 30 minutes before cooking.

Cook in boiling salted water until al dente, 2-4 minutes for fresh pasta, depending on thickness.

---

Turmeric (1 teaspoon) can be added to the dough for color. Semolina flour can substitute for half of the all-purpose flour for a chewier texture.
