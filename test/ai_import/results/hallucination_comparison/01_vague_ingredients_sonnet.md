# Classic Banana Bread

A simple, moist banana bread that's perfect for using up overripe bananas.

Category: Bread

- Ripe bananas
- Flour
- Sugar
- Butter
- Eggs
- Baking soda
- Salt
- Vanilla extract

Mash the bananas. Mix wet ingredients, then fold in dry. Pour into a loaf pan and bake at 350°F until a toothpick comes out clean.
