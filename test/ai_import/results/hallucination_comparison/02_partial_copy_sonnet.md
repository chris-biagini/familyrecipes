# Chicken Parmesan

Makes: 4 servings
Category: Mains

## Prepare the chicken.

- Chicken breasts (boneless, skinless)
- Flour
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs (Italian)
- Parmesan, freshly grated
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Olive oil

## Assemble and top.

- Marinara sauce
- Mozzarella, shredded
- Basil (fresh)
