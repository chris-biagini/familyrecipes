# Classic Vinaigrette

A go-to dressing for any salad. Adjust the ratio to taste.

- Red wine vinegar, 1 tbsp
- Dijon mustard, 1 tsp
- Garlic, 1 small clove: Finely minced.
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Olive oil (extra-virgin), 3 tbsp

Whisk together the red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, garlic, salt, and pepper in a small bowl until combined. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking constantly until the dressing is emulsified and slightly thickened. Taste and adjust seasoning with more salt, pepper, or a tiny splash of vinegar if needed. Use immediately or refrigerate in a jar for up to one week. Shake well before each use.
