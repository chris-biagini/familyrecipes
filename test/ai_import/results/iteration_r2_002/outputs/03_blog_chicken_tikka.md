# Chicken Tikka Masala

Made-from-scratch Chicken Tikka Masala recipe with the signature yoghurt marinated chargrilled chicken smothered in an incredible spice infused curry sauce.

Serves: 4

## Marinate chicken.

- Chicken thighs (boneless, skinless), 600 g: Cubed.
- Yogurt (plain), 1/2 cup
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Minced.
- Ginger (fresh), 1 tbsp: Grated.
- Garam masala, 1 tsp
- Salt, 1 tsp
- Cumin, 1 tsp
- Coriander (ground), 1 tsp
- Paprika, 1 tsp
- Vegetable oil, 1 tbsp
- Cayenne pepper, 1/8 tsp
- Black pepper, Pinch
- Lemon juice, 2 tsp

Combine all marinade ingredients and coat chicken pieces. Cover and refrigerate for a minimum of 3 hours, preferably overnight.

## Sear chicken.

Heat oil in a non-stick pan over high heat until smoking. Sear chicken in batches until charred, 2 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.

## Make sauce.

- Vegetable oil, 3 tbsp
- Butter (unsalted), 30 g
- Onion, 1: Finely chopped.
- Salt, 1 tsp
- Ginger (fresh), 2 tbsp: Grated.
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Crushed.
- Paprika, 1 tbsp
- Turmeric, 2 tsp
- Garam masala, 1 tbsp
- Coriander, 2 tsp
- Cumin, 1 tsp
- Cardamom powder, 1/8 tsp
- Cayenne pepper, 1/8 tsp
- Tomato passata, 1 2/3 cups
- Water, 1 2/3 cups
- Cream, 100 mL
- Sugar, 1 tsp
- Butter (unsalted), 50 g

Wipe pan clean and reduce heat to medium-high. Add oil and butter; sauté onions, ginger, and salt until golden, 5-7 minutes. Add garlic and paprika; cook 2 minutes. Add turmeric, garam masala, coriander, cumin, cardamom powder, and cayenne pepper; cook 2 minutes, stirring. Pour in tomato passata and water; bring to simmer. Cover and simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Blend sauce with stick blender until smooth. Return to pan; add cream, sugar, and butter. Add chicken and simmer until cooked through. Serve over basmati rice.

Basmati rice for serving. Optional cilantro for garnish.
