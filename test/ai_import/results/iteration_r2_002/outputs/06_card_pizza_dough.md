# Pizza Dough

Puffy edges with a chewy crumb, similar to Italian wood-fired pizzerias.

Makes: 3 pizzas
Category: Pizza

## Mix and knead dough.

- Bread flour or pizza flour, 600 g
- Instant yeast, 2 tsp
- Salt, 2 1/2 tsp
- Sugar, 4 tsp
- Olive oil, 4 tbsp
- Water (warm), 330 mL

Mix flour, yeast, salt, and sugar in a large bowl. Add olive oil and warm water and mix until a shaggy dough forms.

Knead by hand for 5 minutes, or use a food processor fitted with dough blade and process for 40 seconds, until dough comes together. The dough will be slightly sticky.

## First rise.

Cover bowl and let dough rise at room temperature for 1-2 hours until doubled in size. Alternatively, refrigerate up to 5 days for flavor development. If refrigerated, remove and let come to room temperature before proceeding.

## Form and second rise.

Divide dough into three equal portions and form into balls. Place on a floured surface or in oiled bowls, cover, and let rise for 1 hour until puffy.

## Stretch, top, and bake.

Stretch each dough ball into a 30 cm (12") round base. Top with sauce, cheese, and preferred toppings. Bake at 275°C for 10 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
