# Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Makes: 1 sandwich

- Sourdough bread, 2 slices: Thick-cut, 1.3-1.5 cm.
- Butter (salted)
- Cheddar (vintage), freshly grated: Or gruyere.
- Mozzarella (fresh), grated

Butter both sides of the bread. Heat a heavy-based skillet over medium heat. Add bread slices and toast lightly. Top each slice with grated vintage cheddar and fresh mozzarella. Cook for approximately 3 minutes per side until the exterior is crispy and the cheese is melted inside.
