# No-Knead Crusty White Bread

Artisan-style bread through an overnight refrigeration method requiring minimal active work.

Makes: 3 loaves
Category: Bread

## Mix dough.

- Flour (all-purpose), 900 g
- Water (lukewarm), 680 g
- Salt, 18 g
- Yeast (instant or active dry), 14 g

Mix all ingredients together until combined into a sticky dough. No kneading required.

## First rise and refrigerate.

Allow dough to rise at room temperature for 2 hours. Transfer to refrigerator and refrigerate for a minimum of 2-7 days before baking.

## Shape and proof.

Tear off approximately one-quarter of the dough. Shape it and let it rise about 60 minutes.

## Bake.

Score the surface. Bake at 450°F for 25-35 minutes with steam.

Based on a recipe from King Arthur Baking. Derives from "Artisan Bread in Five Minutes a Day" by Jeff Hertzberg and Zoë François.
