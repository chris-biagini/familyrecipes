# Flourless Chocolate Brownies

Makes: 16 brownies

## Melt chocolate and butter.

- Dark chocolate (chips or melts), 1 cup
- Butter (unsalted), 85 g

Melt chocolate and butter together in microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring between each interval until smooth.

## Mix batter.

- Vanilla extract, 1 tsp
- Brown sugar, 3/4 cup
- Eggs (large), 2
- Almond meal, 2/3 cup
- Rice flour, 1/3 cup
- Salt, 1/4 tsp

Cool chocolate mixture 5 minutes. Stir in vanilla and brown sugar. Mix in eggs, then almond meal, rice flour, and salt.

## Bake.

Preheat oven to 180°C with rack in lower third. Grease and line a 20 cm square pan. Pour batter into pan and bake 25 minutes until slightly puffed with moist center. Cool 30+ minutes before cutting into squares.
