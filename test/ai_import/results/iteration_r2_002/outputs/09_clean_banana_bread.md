# Banana Bread

This easy banana bread recipe is the best way to use ripe bananas! It's super moist, delicious, and packed with banana flavor.

Makes: 1 loaf
Serves: 8
Category: Baking

## Mix wet ingredients.

- Banana (mashed, very ripe), 2 cups: About 4 large.
- Sugar (cane or brown), 1/2 cup
- Butter (melted) or vegetable oil, 1/2 cup
- Eggs, 2 large
- Vanilla extract, 1 teaspoon

Whisk together mashed banana, sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl.

## Combine dry ingredients and batter.

- Flour (all-purpose), 1 1/2 cups: Spooned and leveled.
- Baking soda, 1 teaspoon
- Sea salt, 1/2 teaspoon
- Cinnamon, 1/2 teaspoon
- Nutmeg, 1/4 teaspoon
- Walnuts (chopped), 1/2 cup

In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, stirring until just combined without overmixing; fold in 1/2 cup walnuts.

## Bake.

- Walnuts, 2 tablespoons: For topping.

Preheat oven to 350°F and grease an 8x4 or 9x5-inch loaf pan. Pour batter into prepared pan and top with remaining 2 tablespoons walnuts. Bake 50-60 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean and top springs back when touched.
