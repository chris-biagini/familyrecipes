# Mom's Chicken Soup

Category: Mains

## Make the broth.

- Chicken (whole), 3-4 lbs
- Water, 10-12 cups
- Onion, 1: Quartered.
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Smashed.

Put chicken in a large pot, cover with water, add onion and garlic. Bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer, skim foam. Cook about 1 1/2 hours until chicken is falling apart.

Remove chicken, cool slightly, then shred meat; discard skin and bones. Strain broth if desired.

## Build the soup.

- Carrots, 2: Sliced.
- Celery, 2 stalks: Sliced.
- Egg noodles, about 1/2 bag
- Dill, a few sprigs
- Salt
- Black pepper

Add carrots and celery to broth; cook about 15 minutes until tender. Return shredded chicken. Season with salt, pepper, and dill.

Cook noodles separately; add to each bowl at serving to prevent mushiness.

---

A squeeze of lemon at the table is optional.
