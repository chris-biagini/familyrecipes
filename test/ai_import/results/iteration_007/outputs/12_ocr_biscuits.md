# Buttermilk Biscuits

Makes: 10 biscuits
Category: Bread

## Make the dough.

- Flour (all-purpose), 2 cups
- Baking powder, 1 tbsp
- Salt, 1/2 tsp
- Butter, 1/3 cup: Cold, cut into small pieces.
- Buttermilk, 3/4 cup

Preheat oven to 425°F. Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Cut in butter with a pastry cutter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add buttermilk; stir just until dough comes together — don't overmix.

## Shape and bake.

Turn dough onto lightly floured surface. Pat to 3/4-inch thickness. Cut with a 2-inch biscuit cutter, pressing straight down without twisting. Place on ungreased baking sheet. Bake 12-15 minutes until golden brown.
