# Beef-and-Bean Chili

Calls for lots of beef.

Serves: 6
Category: Mains

## Brown beef; build chili.

- Ground beef, 4 lb
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Minced.
- Tomato sauce, 2 cans (8 oz each)
- Tomato paste, 1 can (6 oz)
- Chili powder, 1/4 cup
- Cumin, 2 tsp
- Oregano, 2 tsp
- Salt (kosher), 2 tsp
- Cayenne pepper, 1/2 tsp

Brown beef with garlic in a large heavy pot until cooked through. Drain most fat, leaving a little. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, and all spices; stir well. Cover and simmer on low 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Add up to 1 1/2 cups water if liquid runs low.

## Finish and serve.

- Beans (kidney and pinto), 4 cans (15 oz each): Drained and rinsed.
- Masa harina, 1/2 cup

Stir in beans. Whisk masa harina with 1/2 cup water until smooth; stir into chili. Simmer 10 minutes to thicken. Adjust seasoning.

---

Rinse canned beans before using — they can be slimy straight out of the can. Top with shredded cheddar, crumbled bacon, and sliced jalapeños. Substitute regular cornmeal for masa harina. Chili powder, salt, and cayenne may all be adjusted to taste.

Based on a recipe from Ree Drummond.
