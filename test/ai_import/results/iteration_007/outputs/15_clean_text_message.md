# Guacamole

Chunky, not fussy.

Category: Snacks

- Avocados, 2
- Lime, 1/2
- Salt
- Jalapeño, 1/2
- Cilantro: Optional.

Mash avocados with a fork. Squeeze in lime, fold in finely chopped jalapeño and cilantro. Taste; adjust salt and lime. Don't overmix — keep it chunky.

---

Use less jalapeño for less heat.
