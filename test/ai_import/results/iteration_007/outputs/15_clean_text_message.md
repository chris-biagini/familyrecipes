# Guacamole

Category: Snacks
Tags: vegan, quick, easy

- Avocados, 2
- Lime, 1/2
- Salt, a little bit
- Cilantro, some: Optional.
- Jalapeño, 1/2 or less

Mash the avocados with a fork, squeeze the lime in, chop the cilantro and jalapeño real small and mix it all together. Taste it and add more salt/lime if you need to. Don't overmix, it should be chunky.
