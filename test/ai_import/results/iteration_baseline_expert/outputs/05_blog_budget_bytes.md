# Classic Meatloaf

Grandma's version. Actually good.

Serves: 7
Category: Mains
Tags: comfort-food, american

## Mix and shape.

- Egg, 1
- Milk, 1/3 cup
- Worcestershire sauce, 1 tsp
- Breadcrumbs (plain), 1/2 cup
- Italian seasoning, 1 tsp
- Garlic powder, 1/2 tsp
- Salt, 1 tsp
- Black pepper, 1/4 tsp
- Onion, 1/2 cup: Minced.
- Ground beef (80/20), 1 1/2 lbs

Preheat oven to 350°F. Whisk egg, milk, and Worcestershire. Combine breadcrumbs and dry seasonings separately. Add beef, egg mixture, breadcrumb mixture, and onion to large bowl. Mix by hand until just combined — don't overwork.

Shape onto rimmed baking dish into a loaf about 4" wide, 8" long, 2" tall.

## Glaze and bake.

- Ketchup, 1/2 cup
- Sugar (brown), 1 tbsp
- Worcestershire sauce, 1 tbsp
- Yellow mustard, 1 tsp

Stir glaze ingredients together and spread evenly over top and sides of loaf.

Bake 50-55 minutes until internal temp reaches 165°F. Rest 5-10 minutes before slicing.

---

80/20 beef is important — leaner grinds dry out. Brown sugar can be reduced by half if preferred.
