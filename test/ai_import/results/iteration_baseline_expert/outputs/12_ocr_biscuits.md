# Buttermilk Biscuits

Quick and flaky.

Makes: 10 biscuits
Category: Baking
Tags: baked, vegetarian

## Make the dough.

- Flour (all-purpose), 2 cups
- Baking powder, 1 tbsp
- Salt, 1/2 tsp
- Butter, 1/3 cup: Cold, cut into small pieces.
- Buttermilk, 3/4 cup

Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Cut in butter with pastry cutter or two knives until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add buttermilk and stir just until dough comes together — don't overmix.

## Shape and bake.

Preheat oven to 425°F.

Turn dough onto lightly floured surface. Pat to 3/4 inch thickness. Cut with 2-inch biscuit cutter pressing straight down — no twisting. Place on ungreased baking sheet. Bake 12-15 minutes until golden brown.
