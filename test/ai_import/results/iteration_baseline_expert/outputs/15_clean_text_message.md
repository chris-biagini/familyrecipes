# Guacamole

Chunky, simple, classic.

Serves: 2

- Avocados, 2
- Lime, 1/2
- Salt
- Cilantro, some: Finely chopped. Optional.
- Jalapeño, 1/2 or less: Finely chopped.

Mash avocados with a fork. Squeeze in lime, add cilantro and jalapeño, mix. Don't overmix — keep it chunky. Taste and adjust salt and lime.

---

Cilantro is optional. Use less jalapeño to taste.
