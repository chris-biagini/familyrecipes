# Chicken Soup

The cure for everything.

Category: Mains
Tags: comfort-food

## Make the broth.

- Chicken (whole), about 3-4 lbs
- Onion, 1: Quartered.
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Smashed.

Put chicken in large pot, cover with about 10-12 cups water, add onion and garlic. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer, skim foam. Cook about 1.5 hours until chicken is falling apart.

Remove chicken, cool slightly. Shred meat, discard skin and bones. Strain broth if desired.

## Finish the soup.

- Carrots, 2: Sliced.
- Celery, 2 stalks: Sliced.
- Egg noodles, about half a bag
- Dill (fresh), a few sprigs
- Salt
- Black pepper

Add carrots and celery to broth; cook about 15 minutes until tender. Return shredded chicken to pot.

Cook noodles separately; add to each bowl at serving — they get mushy if left in the pot.

Season with salt, pepper, and dill.

---

A squeeze of lemon at the table is a nice addition.
