# Beef Stew

Weekend comfort, low and slow.

Serves: 6
Category: Mains
Tags: comfort-food

## Brown the beef.

- Beef chuck, 2 lbs: Cut into 1-inch cubes.
- Olive oil, 2 tbsp
- Salt
- Black pepper

Season beef with salt and pepper. Heat oil in Dutch oven over medium-high. Brown in batches, about 3 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.

## Build the stew.

- Onion, 1: Diced.
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Minced.
- Carrots, 3: Sliced.
- Potatoes, 3: Cut into chunks.
- Celery, 2 stalks: Sliced.
- Tomatoes (canned, diced), 1 can (14.5 oz)
- Beef broth, 4 cups
- Tomato paste, 1 tbsp
- Thyme (dried), 1 tsp
- Bay leaves, 2

Cook onion in same pot until softened, about 5 minutes. Add garlic, cook 1 minute. Return beef. Add remaining ingredients, bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, simmer 1 1/2-2 hours until beef is tender. Discard bay leaves.
