# Banana Bread

Moist, simple, reliable.

Serves: 8
Category: Baking
Tags: vegetarian, baked, comfort-food

## Mix and bake.

- Banana, 2 cups: Mashed very ripe (about 4 large).
- Sugar (white), 1/2 cup
- Butter (unsalted), 1/2 cup: Melted.
- Egg, 2
- Vanilla extract, 1 tsp
- Flour (all-purpose), 1 1/2 cups
- Baking soda, 1 tsp
- Salt, 1/2 tsp
- Cinnamon, 1/2 tsp
- Nutmeg, 1/4 tsp
- Walnuts, 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp: Chopped.

Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease an 8x4 or 9x5-inch loaf pan.

Whisk banana, sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Add dry to wet, stirring until just combined — don't overmix. Fold in 1/2 cup walnuts.

Pour into prepared pan. Scatter remaining 2 tbsp walnuts on top. Bake 50-60 minutes until toothpick comes out clean and top springs back when pressed.

---

Substitute vegetable oil for the butter. Substitute brown sugar for white.

From Jeanine Donofrio and Phoebe Moore, Love and Lemons.
