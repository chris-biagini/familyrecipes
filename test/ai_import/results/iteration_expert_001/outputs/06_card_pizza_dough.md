# Pizza Dough

Puffy edges, chewy crumb. The real thing.

Makes: 3 pizzas
Category: Pizza
Tags: vegetarian

## Make the dough.

- Flour (bread or pizza), 600 g
- Yeast (instant), 2 tsp
- Salt, 2 1/2 tsp
- Sugar (white), 4 tsp
- Olive oil, 4 tbsp
- Water, 330 ml: Warm.

**By hand:** Combine dry ingredients, add oil and water, mix until shaggy. Knead 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.

**Food processor:** Combine dry ingredients, add oil and water, process 40 seconds.

## Rise and shape.

Cover and rest 1-2 hours until doubled. Divide into 3 balls, rest 1 hour.

Stretch each ball into a 30 cm base. Top with sauce, cheese, and toppings.

## Bake.

Bake at 275°C for 10 minutes.

---

Dough can be refrigerated up to 5 days after the first rise for improved flavor.
