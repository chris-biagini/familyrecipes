# Flourless Chocolate Brownies

Fudgy, no flour needed.

Makes: 16 brownies
Category: Dessert
Tags: gluten-free, baked

- Chocolate chips (dark), 1 cup
- Butter (unsalted), 85 g
- Sugar (brown), 3/4 cup
- Egg, 2
- Almond meal, 2/3 cup
- Rice flour, 1/3 cup
- Vanilla extract, 1 tsp
- Salt, 1/4 tsp

Preheat oven to 180°C. Rack in lower third. Grease and line a 20 cm square pan.

Melt chocolate and butter together in microwave in 30-second intervals. Cool 5 minutes, then stir in vanilla and sugar. Mix in eggs, then almond meal and rice flour.

Pour into pan. Bake 25 minutes until slightly puffed with a moist center. Cool at least 30 minutes before cutting into 16 squares.
