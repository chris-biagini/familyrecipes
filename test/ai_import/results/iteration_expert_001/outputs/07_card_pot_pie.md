# Chicken Pot Pie

Creamy filling, flaky top. Freezes well.

Makes: 4 large or 6 medium pies
Serves: 4
Category: Mains
Tags: comfort-food, make-ahead, freezer-friendly

## Poach the chicken.

- Milk, 2 cups
- Chicken broth, 1 cup
- Stock powder (chicken or vegetable), 2 tsp
- Thyme sprigs, 2: Optional.
- Chicken breast or thighs (boneless), 600 g

Combine milk, broth, and stock powder in large saucepan. Bring to gentle simmer over medium heat. Add chicken and thyme sprigs. Cover and simmer on medium-low 15 minutes — don't boil or milk will split. Remove chicken and shred or dice. Reserve poaching liquid.

## Make the filling.

- Butter, 50 g
- Garlic, 2 cloves: Minced.
- Onion, 1 large: Chopped.
- Thyme (dried), 1 tsp
- Carrots, 2 large: Chopped.
- Celery, 3 ribs: Chopped.
- White wine, 1/3 cup
- Flour, 1/3 cup
- Parmesan, 1/2 cup: Grated.
- Black pepper, 1/2 tsp
- Peas (frozen), 1 cup

Melt butter in large pot over medium-high. Add garlic and onion; cook 2 minutes. Add thyme, carrot, and celery; cook 3 minutes until softened.

Add wine and scrape pot bottom; cook about 1.5 minutes until mostly evaporated. Add flour and stir 1 minute. Add half the poaching liquid, stirring until thick. Add remaining liquid, parmesan, and pepper; stir to combine. Add chicken and peas.

Once steaming, cook 3 minutes, stirring regularly, until thickened. Remove from heat. Spoon into 4 large or 6 small oven-proof pots. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes.

## Top and bake.

- Puff pastry, 2 sheets
- Egg, 1: Whisked.

Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F. Partially thaw pastry. Using a bowl as guide, cut rounds 2.5 cm wider than pots.

Brush pot edges with egg. Lay pastry over each pot, folding down edges to seal. Brush tops with egg. Cut a 2 cm slit in the centre. Bake 35-40 minutes until deep golden. Serve immediately.
