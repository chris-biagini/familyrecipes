# Chicken Tikka Masala

Chargrilled yogurt-marinated chicken in a rich spiced sauce.

Serves: 4
Category: Mains
Tags: indian, comfort-food

## Marinate the chicken.

- Chicken thighs (boneless, skinless), 600 g: Cubed.
- Yogurt (plain), 1/2 cup
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Minced.
- Ginger, 1 tbsp: Grated.
- Garam masala, 1 tsp
- Salt, 1 tsp
- Cumin, 1 tsp
- Coriander, 1 tsp
- Paprika, 1 tsp
- Vegetable oil, 1 tbsp
- Cayenne pepper, 1/8 tsp
- Black pepper, a pinch
- Lemon juice, 2 tsp

Combine all marinade ingredients and coat chicken. Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours, overnight preferred.

## Sear the chicken.

Heat oil in non-stick pan over high until smoking. Sear chicken in batches, about 2 minutes per side until charred. Set aside.

## Make the sauce.

- Vegetable oil, 3 tbsp
- Butter (unsalted), 30 g
- Onion, 1: Finely chopped.
- Salt, 1 tsp
- Ginger, 2 tbsp: Grated.
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Crushed.
- Paprika, 1 tbsp
- Turmeric, 2 tsp
- Garam masala, 1 tbsp
- Coriander, 2 tsp
- Cumin, 1 tsp
- Cardamom, 1/8 tsp
- Cayenne pepper, 1/8 tsp
- Tomato passata, 1 2/3 cups
- Water, 1 2/3 cups
- Cream, 100 ml
- Sugar (white), 1 tsp
- Butter (unsalted), 50 g

Wipe pan clean. Over medium-high, heat oil and 30 g butter. Sauté onion, ginger, and salt until golden, 5-7 minutes. Add garlic and paprika; cook 2 minutes. Add all spices; cook 2 minutes, stirring.

Pour in passata and water, bring to simmer. Cover and simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Blend smooth with stick blender.

Return to heat. Stir in cream, sugar, and 50 g butter. Add chicken and simmer until cooked through.

---

Serve over basmati rice, garnish with cilantro if desired. Butter (unsalted) can be substituted with ghee. Recipe by Nagi, RecipeTin Eats.
