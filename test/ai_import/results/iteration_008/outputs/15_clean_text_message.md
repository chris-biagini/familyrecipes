# Guacamole

Keep it chunky.

Category: Sides

- Avocado, 2
- Lime, 1/2
- Salt
- Jalapeño, 1/2
- Cilantro: Optional.

Mash avocados with a fork. Squeeze in lime, fold in finely chopped jalapeño and cilantro. Don't overmix — keep it chunky. Taste and adjust salt and lime.

---

Use less jalapeño for milder heat.
