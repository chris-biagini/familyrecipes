# Beef-and-Bean Chili

Serves: 6
Category: Mains

## Brown beef; build base.

- Ground beef, 4 lb
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Minced.
- Tomato sauce, 2 cans (8 oz each)
- Tomato paste, 1 can (6 oz)
- Chili powder, 1/4 cup
- Cumin, 2 tsp
- Oregano, 2 tsp
- Salt (kosher), 2 tsp
- Cayenne, 1/2 tsp

Brown ground beef with garlic in a large heavy pot until cooked through. Drain most fat, leaving a little for flavor.

Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and cayenne. Stir well, cover, reduce to low, and simmer 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Add up to 1 1/2 cups water if liquid runs low.

## Add beans; finish and serve.

- Beans (kidney and pinto), 4 cans (15 oz each): Drained and rinsed.
- Masa harina, 1/2 cup
- Cheddar: Shredded.
- Bacon: Crumbled.
- Jalapeños: Sliced.

Stir beans into chili. Mix masa harina with 1/2 cup water until smooth, stir in. Simmer 10 minutes to thicken. Taste and adjust seasoning. Top with cheddar, bacon, and jalapeños.

---

Substitute regular cornmeal for masa harina.

Based on a recipe from Ree Drummond.
