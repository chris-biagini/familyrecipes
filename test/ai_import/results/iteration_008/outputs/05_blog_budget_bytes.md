# Classic Meatloaf

Old-school comfort, exactly as remembered.

Serves: 7
Category: Mains

## Mix and shape loaf.

- Egg, 1
- Milk, 1/3 cup
- Worcestershire sauce, 1 tsp
- Breadcrumbs (plain), 1/2 cup
- Italian seasoning, 1 tsp
- Garlic powder, 1/2 tsp
- Salt, 1 tsp
- Black pepper, 1/4 tsp
- Onion, 1/2 cup: Minced.
- Ground beef (80/20), 1 1/2 lbs

Preheat oven to 350°F. Whisk together egg, milk, and Worcestershire. Combine breadcrumbs, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a separate bowl.

Add beef, egg mixture, breadcrumb mixture, and onion to a large bowl. Mix by hand until just combined — don't overwork.

Shape on a rimmed baking sheet into a loaf about 4" wide × 8" long × 2" tall.

## Glaze and bake.

- Ketchup, 1/2 cup
- Sugar (brown), 1 tbsp
- Worcestershire sauce, 1 tbsp
- Yellow mustard, 1 tsp

Stir glaze ingredients together and spread evenly over top and sides of loaf.

Bake 50-55 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F. Rest 5-10 minutes before slicing.

---

Use 80/20 ground beef — the fat content keeps the loaf from drying out. Reduce brown sugar to 1/2 tbsp if you prefer a less sweet glaze.

Based on a recipe from [Beth Moncel](https://www.budgetbytes.com).
