# Mom's Chicken Soup

Really easy.

Category: Mains

## Simmer chicken.

- Chicken (whole), 3-4 lbs
- Water, 10-12 cups
- Onion, 1: Quartered.
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Smashed.

Put chicken in a large pot. Cover with water, add onion and garlic. Bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer, and skim foam. Cook about 1 1/2 hours until chicken is falling apart.

## Shred chicken; cook vegetables.

- Carrots, 2: Peeled and sliced.
- Celery, 2 stalks: Sliced.

Remove chicken and let cool slightly. Shred meat, discard skin and bones. Strain broth if desired. Add carrots and celery to broth and cook about 15 minutes until tender. Return shredded chicken to pot.

## Finish and serve.

- Egg noodles, about half a bag
- Dill, a few sprigs
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Lemon: Optional.

Cook noodles separately. Season soup with salt, pepper, and dill. Add noodles to each bowl when serving to prevent mushiness.
