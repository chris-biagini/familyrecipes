# Mom's Chicken Soup

Classic comfort, no fuss.

Category: Mains
Tags: comfort-food

## Simmer chicken.

- Chicken (whole), about 3-4 lbs
- Onion, 1: Quartered.
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Smashed.

Place chicken in large pot. Cover with water (about 10-12 cups), add onion and garlic. Bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer, and skim foam. Cook about 1.5 hours until chicken is falling apart.

Remove chicken and let cool slightly. Shred meat; discard skin and bones. Strain broth if desired.

## Add vegetables; finish soup.

- Carrots, 2: Peeled and sliced.
- Celery, 2 stalks: Sliced.
- Dill, a few sprigs
- Salt
- Black pepper

Add carrots and celery to broth. Simmer about 15 minutes until tender. Return shredded chicken to pot. Season with salt, pepper, and dill.

## Cook noodles; serve.

- Egg noodles, about half a bag

Cook noodles separately. Add to each bowl at serving — they turn mushy if left in the soup.

---

A squeeze of lemon at the table is optional.
