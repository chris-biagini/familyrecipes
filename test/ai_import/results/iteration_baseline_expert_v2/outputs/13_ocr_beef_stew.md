# Beef Stew

Built for a cold afternoon.

Serves: 6
Category: Mains
Tags: comfort-food

## Brown beef.

- Beef chuck, 2 lbs: Cut into 1-inch cubes.
- Olive oil, 2 tbsp
- Salt
- Black pepper

Season beef with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium-high. Brown in batches, about 3 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.

## Build and simmer.

- Onion, 1: Diced.
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Minced.
- Carrots, 3: Peeled and sliced.
- Potatoes, 3: Cut into chunks.
- Celery, 2 stalks: Sliced.
- Tomatoes (canned, diced), 1 can (14.5 oz)
- Beef broth, 4 cups
- Tomato paste, 1 tbsp
- Thyme, 1 tsp
- Bay leaves, 2

Add onion to pot; cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook 1 minute more. Return beef to pot. Add remaining ingredients, bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer 1 1/2-2 hours until beef is tender. Remove bay leaves before serving.
