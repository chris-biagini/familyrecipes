# Classic Meatloaf

Tender, glazed, done right.

Category: Mains
Tags: comfort-food, american

## Mix and shape.

- Egg, 1
- Milk, 1/3 cup
- Worcestershire sauce, 1 tsp
- Breadcrumbs (plain), 1/2 cup
- Italian seasoning, 1 tsp
- Garlic powder, 1/2 tsp
- Salt, 1 tsp
- Black pepper, 1/4 tsp
- Onion, 1/2 cup: Minced.
- Ground beef (80/20), 1 1/2 lbs

Whisk egg, milk, and Worcestershire in a small bowl. In a separate bowl, combine breadcrumbs, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Add beef, egg mixture, breadcrumb mixture, and onion to a large bowl; mix by hand until just combined — don't overwork. Shape into a loaf about 4×8×2 inches on a rimmed baking sheet.

## Make glaze; bake.

- Ketchup, 1/2 cup
- Sugar (brown), 1 tbsp
- Worcestershire sauce, 1 tbsp
- Mustard (yellow), 1 tsp

Preheat oven to 350°F. Stir together glaze ingredients and spread over top and sides of loaf. Bake 50-55 minutes until internal temp reaches 165°F. Rest 5-10 minutes before slicing.

---

Use 80/20 ground beef — the fat adds flavor and keeps the loaf from drying out. Reduce sugar (brown) to 1/2 tbsp if preferred; ketchup is already sweet.

Based on a recipe from Beth Moncel.
