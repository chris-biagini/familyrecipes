# Guacamole

Keep it chunky.

Category: Snacks
Tags: vegan

- Avocados, 2
- Lime, 1/2
- Salt
- Jalapeño, 1/2: Minced.
- Cilantro: Optional.

Mash avocados with a fork — keep it chunky. Squeeze in lime, stir in jalapeño and cilantro. Taste; adjust salt and lime.

---

Use less jalapeño for milder heat.
