# Beef-and-Bean Chili

Proper chili, no shortcuts.

Serves: 6
Category: Mains
Tags: comfort-food, american

## Brown beef.

- Ground beef, 4 lb
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Minced.

Brown beef with garlic in a large heavy pot until cooked through. Drain off most of the fat, leaving a little for flavor.

## Simmer; add beans and thickener.

- Tomato sauce, 2 cans (8 oz each)
- Tomato paste, 1 can (6 oz)
- Chili powder, 1/4 cup
- Cumin, 2 tsp
- Oregano, 2 tsp
- Salt (kosher), 2 tsp
- Cayenne, 1/2 tsp
- Beans (kidney and pinto), 4 cans (15 oz each): Drained and rinsed.
- Masa harina, 1/2 cup

Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and cayenne. Cover and simmer on low for 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Add up to 1 1/2 cups water if the level runs low.

Stir in beans. Whisk masa harina with 1/2 cup water until smooth, then stir into chili. Simmer 10 more minutes to thicken. Adjust seasoning.

---

Shredded cheddar, crumbled bacon, and sliced jalapeños for topping. Can substitute regular cornmeal for masa harina.

Based on a recipe from [Ree Drummond](https://www.thepioneerwoman.com).
