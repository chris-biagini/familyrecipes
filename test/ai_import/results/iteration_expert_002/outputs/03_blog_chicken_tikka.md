# Chicken Tikka Masala

Chargrilled yogurt-marinated chicken in a rich spiced tomato cream sauce.

Serves: 4
Category: Mains
Tags: indian, comfort-food

## Marinate the chicken.

- Chicken thighs (boneless, skinless), 600 g: Cubed.
- Yogurt (plain), 1/2 cup
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Minced.
- Ginger, 1 tbsp: Grated.
- Garam masala, 1 tsp
- Salt, 1 tsp
- Cumin, 1 tsp
- Coriander, 1 tsp
- Paprika, 1 tsp
- Vegetable oil, 1 tbsp
- Cayenne pepper, 1/8 tsp
- Black pepper, a pinch
- Lemon juice, 2 tsp

Combine all marinade ingredients, coat chicken, cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours (overnight preferred).

## Sear the chicken.

Heat oil in non-stick pan over high until smoking. Sear chicken in batches until charred, about 2 minutes per side. Set aside.

## Make the sauce.

- Vegetable oil, 3 tbsp
- Butter (unsalted), 30 g
- Onion, 1: Finely chopped.
- Salt, 1 tsp
- Ginger, 2 tbsp: Grated.
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Crushed.
- Paprika, 1 tbsp
- Turmeric, 2 tsp
- Garam masala, 1 tbsp
- Coriander, 2 tsp
- Cumin, 1 tsp
- Cardamom, 1/8 tsp
- Cayenne pepper, 1/8 tsp
- Tomato passata, 1 2/3 cups
- Water, 1 2/3 cups
- Cream, 100 ml
- Sugar (white), 1 tsp
- Butter (unsalted), 50 g

Wipe pan clean, reduce to medium-high. Heat oil and 30 g butter; sauté onion, ginger, and salt until golden, 5-7 minutes. Add garlic and paprika; cook 2 minutes. Add all spices; cook 2 minutes, stirring.

Pour in passata and water, bring to simmer, cover and cook 15 minutes. Blend smooth with stick blender. Return to heat; stir in cream, sugar, and remaining 50 g butter. Add chicken and simmer until cooked through.

---

Serve over basmati rice, with cilantro if desired. Overnight marinating gives noticeably better char and flavor. Recipe by Nagi of RecipeTin Eats.
