# Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

Worth the overnight wait.

Makes: 16 cookies
Category: Baking
Tags: vegetarian, baked

## Mix dough.

- Flour (all-purpose), 281 g
- Baking soda, 1 tsp
- Cornstarch, 1 1/2 tsp
- Salt, 1/2 tsp
- Butter (unsalted), 170 g: Melted and cooled 5 minutes.
- Sugar (brown), 150 g: Packed.
- Sugar (white), 100 g
- Egg, 1: Room temperature.
- Egg yolk, 1: Room temperature.
- Vanilla extract, 2 tsp
- Chocolate chips (semi-sweet), 225 g

Whisk flour, baking soda, cornstarch, and salt together in large bowl. In separate bowl, whisk melted butter with both sugars until no lumps remain. Whisk in egg and yolk, then vanilla. Pour into dry ingredients and mix with spatula. Fold in chocolate chips.

Cover tightly and refrigerate at least 2 hours or up to 3 days.

## Bake.

Preheat oven to 325°F. Line baking sheets with parchment. If dough chilled longer than 2 hours, rest at room temperature about 15 minutes.

Scoop about 3 tbsp dough per cookie, roll into a ball, then shape into a cylinder. Arrange 3 inches apart on prepared sheets.

Bake 13-14 minutes until edges are lightly browned — centers will look underdone. Cool on baking sheet 10 minutes before transferring to rack.

---

Makes 16 XL or 20 medium-large cookies depending on scoop size. Chilling overnight prevents overspreading. Store tightly covered at room temperature up to 1 week.
