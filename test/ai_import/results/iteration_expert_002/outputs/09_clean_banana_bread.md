# Banana Bread

Moist, simple, no fuss.

Serves: 8
Category: Baking
Tags: vegetarian, baked, comfort-food

## Mix wet ingredients.

- Banana, 2 cups: Mashed, very ripe (about 4 large).
- Sugar (white), 1/2 cup
- Butter (unsalted), 1/2 cup: Melted.
- Egg, 2
- Vanilla extract, 1 tsp

Whisk together in large bowl.

## Mix dry ingredients and bake.

- Flour (all-purpose), 1 1/2 cups
- Baking soda, 1 tsp
- Salt, 1/2 tsp
- Cinnamon, 1/2 tsp
- Nutmeg, 1/4 tsp
- Walnuts, 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp: Chopped.

Grease an 8x4 or 9x5-inch loaf pan. Preheat oven to 350°F.

Whisk dry ingredients together. Add to wet, stirring until just combined — don't overmix. Fold in 1/2 cup walnuts.

Pour into pan, top with remaining 2 tbsp walnuts. Bake 50-60 minutes until toothpick comes out clean and top springs back.

---

Can substitute vegetable oil for butter. Can substitute brown sugar for white.

From Jeanine Donofrio and Phoebe Moore / Love and Lemons.
