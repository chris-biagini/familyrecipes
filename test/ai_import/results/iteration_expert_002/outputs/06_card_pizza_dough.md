# Pizza Dough

Puffy edges, chewy crumb — proper Italian-style.

Makes: 3 pizzas
Category: Pizza
Tags: vegan

## Make the dough.

- Flour (bread or pizza), 600 g
- Yeast (instant), 2 tsp
- Salt, 2 1/2 tsp
- Sugar (white), 4 tsp
- Olive oil, 4 tbsp
- Water, 330 ml: Warm.

**By hand:** Combine dry ingredients, add oil and water, mix to shaggy dough. Knead 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.

**Food processor:** Pulse dry ingredients, add oil and water, run 40 seconds.

## Rise and form.

Cover and rest 1-2 hours until doubled. Divide into 3 balls. Second rise: 1 hour.

Stretch each ball into a 30 cm base. Top with sauce, cheese, and toppings of choice.

## Bake.

Bake at 275°C for 10 minutes.

---

Recipe by Nagi at RecipeTin Eats. Refrigerate dough after first rise for up to 5 days — longer cold fermentation develops flavor.
