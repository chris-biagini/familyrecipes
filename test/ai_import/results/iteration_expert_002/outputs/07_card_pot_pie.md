# Chicken Pot Pie

Creamy filling, flaky top. Freezes well.

Makes: 4-6 pies
Serves: 6
Category: Mains
Tags: comfort-food, make-ahead, freezer-friendly

## Poach the chicken.

- Chicken breasts (boneless), 600 g
- Milk, 2 cups
- Chicken broth, 1 cup
- Stock powder, 2 tsp
- Thyme sprigs, 2: Optional.

Combine milk, broth, and stock powder in large saucepan; bring to gentle simmer over medium heat. Add chicken and thyme sprigs. Cover and simmer on medium-low 15 minutes — don't boil or milk will split. Remove chicken and shred or dice; reserve poaching liquid.

## Make the filling.

- Butter, 50 g
- Onion, 1 large: Chopped.
- Garlic, 2 cloves: Minced.
- Thyme (dried), 1 tsp
- Carrots, 2 large: Chopped.
- Celery, 3 ribs: Chopped.
- White wine, 1/3 cup
- Flour, 1/3 cup
- Parmesan, 1/2 cup: Grated.
- Black pepper, 1/2 tsp
- Peas (frozen), 1 cup

Melt butter in large pot over medium-high. Cook garlic and onion 2 minutes. Add thyme, carrot, and celery; cook 3 minutes until softened.

Add wine, scraping bottom; cook about 1.5 minutes until mostly evaporated. Add flour and stir 1 minute. Add half the poaching liquid, stirring until flour is fully incorporated. Add remaining liquid, parmesan, and pepper; stir to combine.

Add chicken and peas. Once steaming, cook 3 minutes, stirring regularly, until thickened. Remove from heat. Divide into 4 large or 6 small oven-proof pots. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes.

## Assemble and bake.

- Puff pastry, 2 sheets
- Egg, 1: Whisked.

Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F. Partially thaw pastry. Cut rounds about 2.5 cm wider than pots, using a bowl as guide. Brush pot edges with egg, top with pastry rounds, and fold edges down. Brush tops with egg and cut a 2 cm slit in the center. Bake 35-40 minutes until deep golden. Serve immediately.

---

Recipe by Nagi, RecipeTin Eats. Chicken thighs can be substituted for breasts. Thyme sprigs are optional.
