# Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Worth every minute.

Serves: 1
Category: Mains
Tags: vegetarian, quick

## Assemble.

- Bread (sourdough), 2 slices: Thick-cut, 1.3-1.5 cm.
- Butter (salted): For spreading.
- Cheddar (vintage), freshly grated
- Mozzarella (fresh), grated

Butter both sides of each bread slice. Lightly toast in heavy skillet, then add cheese.

Cook about 3 minutes per side until exterior is crispy and cheese is fully melted.

---

Recipe by Nagi, RecipeTin Eats. Gruyere can substitute for the vintage cheddar.
