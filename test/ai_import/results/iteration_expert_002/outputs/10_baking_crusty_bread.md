# No-Knead Crusty White Bread

Fridge does the work.

Makes: 4 loaves
Category: Bread
Tags: make-ahead

## Mix and refrigerate.

- Flour (all-purpose), 900 g
- Water, 680 g: Lukewarm.
- Salt (table), 18 g
- Yeast (instant or active dry), 14 g

Mix all ingredients into a sticky dough. Let rise at room temperature 2 hours, then refrigerate 2-7 days.

## Shape and bake.

Tear off about one-quarter of the dough, shape, and let rise about 60 minutes. Score surface. Bake at 450°F with steam for 25-35 minutes.

---

Adapted from *Artisan Bread in Five Minutes a Day* by Jeff Hertzberg and Zoë François. Imperial equivalents: 7 1/2 cups flour, 3 cups water, 1 tbsp salt, 1 1/2 tbsp yeast.
