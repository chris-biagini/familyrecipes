# Roast Chicken

Simple, buttery, crispy-skinned perfection.

Serves: 5
Category: Mains
Tags: roasted

## Make garlic-herb-lemon butter.

- Butter (unsalted), 100 g: Melted.
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Minced.
- Sage, 1 tbsp: Finely chopped.
- Rosemary, 2 tsp: Finely chopped.
- Parsley, 1 tbsp: Finely chopped.
- Salt, 1/2 tsp
- Black pepper, 1/2 tsp
- Lemon, 1: Quartered.

Mix butter with garlic, herbs, salt, and pepper. Squeeze in juice from 2 lemon wedges; reserve the squeezed wedges.

## Prepare and stuff chicken.

- Chicken (whole), 1.75-2 kg
- Rosemary, 3 sprigs
- Salt
- Black pepper

Remove chicken from fridge 30 minutes before cooking. Loosen skin over breast and drumsticks using a spoon. Prop upright and work herb butter generously under skin, reserving some for the surface. Drizzle remaining butter over top and squeeze remaining 2 lemon wedges over the surface. Stuff cavity with the squeezed lemon wedges and rosemary sprigs. Tie drumsticks, tuck wing tips under. Season surface with salt and pepper.

## Roast.

- Olive oil, 2 tsp
- White wine (dry), 1 cup: Or low-sodium chicken broth.
- Onion, 1: Quartered.
- Garlic, 1 bulb: Halved horizontally.

Preheat oven to 220°C/450°F (200°C/430°F fan). Place onion and garlic in roasting pan, set chicken on top, pour wine around it, and drizzle chicken with olive oil.

Roast 10 minutes at high heat, then reduce to 180°C/350°F and roast 1 hour 15 minutes, basting at 30 minutes and 1 hour. Internal temp should reach 75°C/165°F.

Rest uncovered 15 minutes (uncovered keeps skin crispy). Serve with pan juices. The roasted garlic is excellent alongside; discard onion.
