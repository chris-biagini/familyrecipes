# Chicken Soup

Liquid comfort.

Category: Mains
Tags: comfort-food

## Make broth.

- Chicken (whole), 1 (about 3-4 lbs)
- Onion, 1: Quartered.
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Smashed.
- Water, 10-12 cups

Place chicken in a large pot with onion and garlic. Cover with water. Bring to a boil, skim foam, then reduce to a simmer. Cook about 1.5 hours until falling apart.

Remove chicken and let cool. Shred meat; discard skin and bones. Strain broth if desired.

## Add vegetables; finish.

- Carrots, 2: Peeled and sliced.
- Celery, 2 stalks: Sliced.
- Egg noodles, about half a bag
- Dill, a few sprigs
- Salt
- Black pepper

Return broth to heat. Add carrots and celery; cook about 15 minutes until tender. Add shredded chicken back to the pot. Season with salt, pepper, and dill.

Cook noodles separately; add to each bowl at serving.

---

Lemon squeeze at the table is optional. Keep noodles separate from leftover soup — they get mushy stored in the broth.
