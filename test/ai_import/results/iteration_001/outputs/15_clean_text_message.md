# Guacamole

Chunky and bright.

Category: Snacks
Tags: vegan

- Avocados, 2
- Lime, 1/2
- Salt
- Cilantro: Optional.
- Jalapeño, 1/2: Minced.

Mash avocados with a fork — keep it chunky. Squeeze in lime, fold in cilantro and jalapeño, season with salt. Taste and adjust salt and lime.

---

Use less jalapeño for milder heat.
