# Beef and Bean Chili

Serves: 6
Category: Mains
Tags: comfort-food, american

## Brown beef.

- Ground beef, 4 lb
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Minced.

Brown beef with garlic in a large heavy pot. Drain most of the fat, leaving a little behind.

## Simmer.

- Tomato sauce, 2 cans (8 oz each)
- Tomato paste, 1 can (6 oz)
- Chili powder, 1/4 cup
- Cumin, 2 tsp
- Oregano, 2 tsp
- Salt (kosher), 2 tsp
- Cayenne, 1/2 tsp

Add all ingredients and stir well. Cover and simmer on low for 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Add up to 1 1/2 cups water if liquid runs low.

## Finish and serve.

- Beans (kidney and pinto, canned), 4 cans (15 oz each): Drained and rinsed.
- Masa harina, 1/2 cup
- Cheddar: Shredded.
- Bacon: Crumbled.
- Jalapeños: Sliced.

Stir beans into chili. Mix masa harina with 1/2 cup water until smooth, then stir in. Simmer 10 minutes to thicken. Adjust seasoning. Top with cheese, bacon, and jalapeños.

---

Substitute cornmeal for masa harina. Cheddar, bacon, and jalapeños are toppings — add as desired.

Based on a recipe from [Ree Drummond](https://www.thepioneerwoman.com).
