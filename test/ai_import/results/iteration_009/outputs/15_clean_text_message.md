# Guacamole

Keep it chunky.

Category: Snacks

- Avocados, 2
- Lime, 1/2
- Salt
- Jalapeño, 1/2: Finely chopped.
- Cilantro: Finely chopped. Optional.

Mash avocados with a fork. Squeeze in lime juice, stir in jalapeño and cilantro, season with salt. Fold to combine — keep texture rough. Taste and adjust salt and lime.

---

Use less than half a jalapeño for milder heat.
