# Mom's Chicken Soup

Category: Mains
Tags: easy

## Make the broth.

- Chicken (whole), 1 (3-4 lbs)
- Water, about 10-12 cups
- Onion, 1: Quartered.
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Smashed.

Cover chicken with water in a large pot, add onion and garlic. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer, skim foam. Cook about 1.5 hours until chicken is falling apart.

## Shred chicken; add vegetables.

- Carrots, 2: Peeled and sliced.
- Celery, 2 stalks: Sliced.

Remove chicken, cool slightly, shred meat, discard skin and bones. Strain broth if desired. Add carrots and celery; cook about 15 minutes until tender. Return shredded chicken to pot.

## Finish and serve.

- Dill, a few sprigs
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Egg noodles, about half a bag
- Lemon: Optional.

Cook noodles separately; add to each bowl when serving (they get mushy if left in the soup). Season with salt, pepper, and dill.

---

Lemon is optional; add a squeeze at the table.
