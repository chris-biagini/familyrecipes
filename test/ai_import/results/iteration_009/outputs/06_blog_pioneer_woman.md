# Beef-and-Bean Chili

A proper chili calls for lots of beef.

Serves: 6
Category: Mains

## Brown beef; build chili.

- Ground beef, 4 lb
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Minced.
- Tomato sauce, 2 cans (8 oz each)
- Tomato paste, 1 can (6 oz)
- Chili powder, 1/4 cup
- Cumin, 2 tsp
- Oregano, 2 tsp
- Salt (kosher), 2 tsp
- Cayenne pepper, 1/2 tsp

In a large heavy pot, brown beef with garlic until cooked through. Drain most of the fat, leaving a little behind.

Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and cayenne. Stir well, cover, and reduce heat to low. Simmer 1 hour, stirring occasionally. If liquid runs low, add up to 1 1/2 cups water.

## Finish and serve.

- Beans (kidney and pinto), 4 cans (15 oz each): Drained and rinsed.
- Masa harina, 1/2 cup
- Cheddar
- Bacon
- Jalapeños

Stir beans into chili. Mix masa harina with 1/2 cup water until smooth, then stir into chili. Simmer 10 minutes to thicken. Adjust seasonings. Top with cheddar, bacon, and jalapeños.

---

Cheddar, bacon, and jalapeños are suggested toppings. Substitute cornmeal for the masa harina.

Based on a recipe from Ree Drummond.
