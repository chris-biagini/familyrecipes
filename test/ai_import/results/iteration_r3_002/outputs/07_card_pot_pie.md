# Chicken Pot Pie

A creamy chicken and vegetable filling with thyme, topped with flaky puff pastry.

Serves: 4
Category: Mains

## Poach the chicken.

- Chicken breast, 600 g
- Milk, 2 cups
- Chicken broth, 1 cup
- Stock powder (chicken or vegetable), 2 tsp
- Thyme, 2 sprigs: Optional.

Place milk, broth, and stock powder in large saucepan; bring to gentle simmer on medium heat. Add chicken and thyme sprigs. Cover and simmer gently on medium-low for 15 minutes (avoid boiling to prevent milk separation). Remove chicken, shred or dice (some uncooked parts are fine). Set poaching liquid aside.

## Make the filling.

- Butter, 50 g
- Garlic, 2 cloves: Minced.
- Onion, 1 large: Chopped.
- Thyme (dried), 1 tsp
- Carrots, 2 large: Chopped.
- Celery, 3 ribs: Chopped.
- White wine, 1/3 cup
- Flour, 1/3 cup
- Parmesan, 1/2 cup: Grated.
- Black pepper, 1/2 tsp
- Peas (frozen), 1 cup

Melt butter in large pot over medium-high heat. Add garlic and onion; cook 2 minutes. Add thyme, carrot, and celery; cook 3 minutes until softened. Add wine, stir, scraping pot bottom; cook 1.5 minutes until mostly evaporated. Add flour and stir for 1 minute. Add half the reserved poaching liquid, stirring until flour incorporates into thick sludge. Add remaining milk broth, parmesan, and pepper. Stir. Add chicken and peas; stir. Once heated (steam visible), cook 3 minutes, stirring regularly to thicken. Remove from heat. Spoon into oven-proof pots (4 large or 6 small). Cool in fridge at least 30 minutes.

## Assemble and bake.

- Puff pastry, 2 sheets
- Egg, 1: Whisked.

Partially thaw puff pastry. Cut rounds 2.5 cm wider than pots using a bowl as guide. Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F. Brush pot edges with egg. Top with pastry, folding down edges. Brush pastry with egg. Cut 2 cm slit in center with small knife. Bake 35-40 minutes until deep golden. Serve immediately.

Recipe by Nagi, RecipeTin Eats. Can substitute boneless thighs for chicken breast. Thyme sprigs are optional.
