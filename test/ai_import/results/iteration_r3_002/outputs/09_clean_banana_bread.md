# Banana Bread

Makes: 1 loaf
Serves: 8
Category: Baking

## Mix the batter.

- Banana, 2 cups: Mashed very ripe.
- Sugar (white), 1/2 cup
- Butter, 1/2 cup: Melted.
- Egg, 2
- Vanilla extract, 1 tsp
- Flour (all-purpose), 1 1/2 cups
- Baking soda, 1 tsp
- Salt (sea), 1/2 tsp
- Cinnamon, 1/2 tsp
- Nutmeg, 1/4 tsp
- Walnuts, 1/2 cup: Chopped.

Preheat oven to 350°F and grease an 8x4 or 9x5-inch loaf pan.

Whisk together mashed banana, sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl. In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, stirring until just combined without overmixing; fold in 1/2 cup walnuts.

## Bake.

- Walnuts, 2 tbsp: Chopped.

Pour batter into prepared pan and top with remaining 2 tablespoons walnuts. Bake 50-60 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean and top springs back when touched.

---

Substitute brown sugar for the white sugar. Substitute vegetable oil for the butter. Flour should be spooned and leveled.

Recipe by Jeanine Donofrio and Phoebe Moore, Love and Lemons.
