# Chicken Tikka Masala

Made-from-scratch Chicken Tikka Masala recipe with the signature yoghurt marinated chargrilled chicken smothered in an incredible spice infused curry sauce.

Serves: 4
Category: Mains

## Marinate the chicken.

- Chicken thighs (boneless, skinless), 600 g: Cubed.
- Yogurt (plain), 1/2 cup
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Minced.
- Ginger, 1 tbsp: Grated.
- Garam masala, 1 tsp
- Salt, 1 tsp
- Cumin, 1 tsp
- Coriander, 1 tsp
- Paprika, 1 tsp
- Vegetable oil, 1 tbsp
- Cayenne pepper, 1/8 tsp
- Black pepper, pinch
- Lemon juice, 2 tsp

Combine marinade ingredients and coat chicken pieces. Cover and refrigerate minimum 3 hours (overnight preferred).

Heat oil in non-stick pan over high heat until smoking. Sear chicken in batches until charred, 2 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.

## Make the sauce.

- Vegetable oil, 3 tbsp
- Butter (unsalted), 30 g: Or ghee.
- Onion, 1: Finely chopped.
- Salt, 1 tsp
- Ginger, 2 tbsp: Grated.
- Garlic, 6 cloves: Crushed.
- Paprika, 1 tbsp
- Turmeric, 2 tsp
- Garam masala, 1 tbsp
- Coriander, 2 tsp
- Cumin, 1 tsp
- Cardamom, 1/8 tsp
- Cayenne pepper, 1/8 tsp
- Tomato passata, 1 2/3 cups
- Water, 1 2/3 cups
- Cream, 100 ml
- Sugar (white), 1 tsp
- Butter (unsalted), 50 g: Or ghee.
- Basmati rice: For serving.

Wipe pan clean, reduce heat to medium-high. Add oil and butter; sauté onions, ginger, and salt until golden, 5-7 minutes. Add garlic and paprika; cook 2 minutes. Add spices; cook 2 minutes, stirring. Pour in tomato passata and water; bring to simmer. Cover and simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Blend sauce with stick blender until smooth. Return to pan; add cream, sugar, and butter. Add chicken and simmer until cooked through. Serve over basmati rice with optional cilantro.

---

Recipe by Nagi, RecipeTin Eats. Substitute ghee for the butter. Cilantro is optional as a garnish.
