# Pizza Dough

Puffy edges with a chewy crumb, similar to Italian wood-fired pizzerias.

Makes: 3 pizzas
Category: Pizza

## Make the dough.

- Flour (bread or pizza), 600 g
- Yeast (instant), 2 tsp
- Salt, 2 1/2 tsp
- Sugar (white), 4 tsp
- Olive oil, 4 tbsp
- Water, 330 ml: Warm.

Mix dry ingredients, then add wet components. Knead by hand for 5 minutes, or use a food processor for 40 seconds.

## Rise and shape.

Allow dough to rise for 1-2 hours until doubled. Form into three dough balls and allow a second rise of 1 hour. Stretch into 30 cm bases.

## Top and bake.

Top with sauce, cheese, and preferred toppings. Bake at 275°C for 10 minutes.

---

Dough can be refrigerated for up to 5 days after the first rise for flavor development. Recipe by Nagi at RecipeTin Eats.
