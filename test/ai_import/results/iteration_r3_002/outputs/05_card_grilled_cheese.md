# Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Makes: 1 sandwich
Serves: 1
Category: Mains

- Bread (sourdough), 2 slices: Thick-cut (1.3-1.5 cm).
- Butter (salted): For spreading.
- Cheddar (vintage), freshly grated
- Mozzarella (fresh), grated

Butter both sides of the bread. Lightly toast, then add cheese. Cook approximately 3 minutes per side to achieve a crispy exterior while keeping the cheese melted inside.

---

From Nagi at RecipeTin Eats. Can substitute gruyere for the vintage cheddar.
