# No-Knead Crusty White Bread

Makes: 4 loaves
Category: Bread

## Mix and refrigerate.

- Flour (all-purpose), 900 g
- Water, 680 g: Lukewarm.
- Salt, 18 g
- Yeast, 14 g: Instant or active dry.

Mix ingredients into a sticky dough. Allow to rise at room temperature for two hours, then refrigerate for a minimum of 2-7 days.

## Shape and bake.

When ready to bake, tear off approximately one-quarter of the dough, shape it, and let it rise about 60 minutes. Score the surface and bake at 450°F for 25-35 minutes with steam.

Adapted from "Artisan Bread in Five Minutes a Day" by Jeff Hertzberg and Zoë François. Recipe of the Year for King Arthur Baking in both 2016 and 2026.
