# Pizza Dough

Makes: 3 pizzas
Category: Pizza

## Make the dough.

- Flour (bread or pizza), 600 g
- Yeast (instant), 2 tsp
- Salt, 2 1/2 tsp
- Sugar (white), 4 tsp
- Olive oil, 4 tbsp
- Water, 330 ml: Warm.

Mix dry ingredients, then add wet components. Knead by hand for 5 minutes, or use a food processor for 40 seconds.

## First rise.

Allow dough to rise for 1-2 hours until doubled.

## Second rise and bake.

Form dough into three balls and allow to rise for 1 hour. Stretch each into a 30 cm base. Top with sauce, cheese, and preferred toppings. Bake at 275°C for 10 minutes.

---

Dough can be refrigerated for up to 5 days after the first rise for flavor development. The dough produces puffy edges with a chewy crumb, similar to Italian wood-fired pizzerias. Recipe by Nagi, RecipeTin Eats.
