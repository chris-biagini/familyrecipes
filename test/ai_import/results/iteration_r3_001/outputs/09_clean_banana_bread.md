# Banana Bread

Makes: 1 loaf
Serves: 8
Category: Baking

## Mix wet ingredients.

- Banana, 2 cups: Mashed, very ripe.
- Sugar (white), 1/2 cup
- Butter, 1/2 cup: Melted.
- Egg, 2
- Vanilla extract, 1 tsp

Preheat oven to 350°F and grease an 8x4 or 9x5-inch loaf pan. Whisk together mashed banana, sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl.

## Combine and bake.

- Flour (all-purpose), 1 1/2 cups: Spooned and leveled.
- Baking soda, 1 tsp
- Salt, 1/2 tsp
- Cinnamon, 1/2 tsp
- Nutmeg, 1/4 tsp
- Walnuts, 1/2 cup plus 2 tbsp: Chopped.

In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, stirring until just combined without overmixing; fold in 1/2 cup walnuts. Pour batter into prepared pan and top with remaining 2 tablespoons walnuts. Bake 50-60 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean and top springs back when touched.

---

Substitute brown sugar for white sugar. Substitute vegetable oil for butter.

From Love and Lemons, by Jeanine Donofrio and Phoebe Moore.
