# Mom's Chicken Soup

Mom's go-to.

Category: Mains
Tags: easy

## Simmer the chicken.

- Chicken (whole), 1 (about 3-4 lbs)
- Water, about 10-12 cups
- Onion, 1: Quartered.
- Garlic, 3 cloves: Smashed.

Put chicken in large pot, cover with water. Add onion and garlic. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer, and skim foam. Cook about 1.5 hours until chicken is falling apart.

## Shred chicken; add vegetables.

- Carrots, 2: Sliced.
- Celery, 2 stalks: Sliced.

Remove chicken and let cool. Shred meat; discard skin and bones. Strain broth if desired. Add carrots and celery to broth; cook about 15 min until tender. Return shredded chicken to pot.

## Cook noodles; finish and serve.

- Egg noodles, about half a bag
- Dill, a few sprigs
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Lemon: Optional.

Cook noodles separately. Season soup with salt, pepper, and dill. Add noodles to each bowl just before serving — they get mushy if left in the soup.

---

Lemon squeeze at the table is optional.
