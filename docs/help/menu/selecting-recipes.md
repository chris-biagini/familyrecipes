# Selecting Recipes

On the Menu page, recipes and QuickBites are listed by category. Check the
ones you're planning to make this week.

Your selections are shared with everyone in the kitchen — if another
household member checks a recipe, you'll see it checked too.

## Ingredient availability

Each recipe row shows an availability indicator — a quick way to see how
many of its ingredients you already have on hand (based on your Groceries
On Hand list).

- **Single dot, filled**: you have the ingredient
- **Single dot, hollow**: you don't have it
- **`x/y` fraction**: `x` of `y` ingredients are on hand — click to expand and see which ones

The fraction fades out when fewer ingredients are available, so at-a-glance
you can see which recipes you're almost ready to make.

## Deselecting

Unchecking a recipe removes its ingredients from the grocery list. If you
later recheck it, its ingredients return.

When you deselect a recipe, its ingredients may be *pruned* — set aside
until the next time they're needed. See
[How the system learns]({{ site.baseurl }}/groceries/learning/) for details.
