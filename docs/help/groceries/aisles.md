# Editing Aisles

Ingredients are grouped by aisle on the grocery list. Aisle assignments
come from the [ingredient catalog]({{ site.baseurl }}/ingredients/nutrition-data/)
— you can set or change the aisle for any ingredient there.

## Reordering aisles

Click **Edit Aisles** on the Groceries page to open the aisle editor.
Drag aisles up or down to change the order they appear on the list.
Reordering to match your store's layout can make shopping faster.

## Renaming aisles

Click an aisle name to edit it. Renaming an aisle updates all ingredients
assigned to that aisle automatically.

## Adding and removing aisles

Type a new name in the input at the bottom of the editor and press Enter
to add it. Click the × on an aisle to remove it — ingredients in that aisle
move to Miscellaneous.

## Miscellaneous

Items without an aisle assignment (including most custom items) land in
**Miscellaneous**, which always appears last.
