# How the Grocery List Works

The Groceries page builds your shopping list from the recipes and QuickBites
you select on the [Menu]({{ site.baseurl }}/menu/) page.

Every ingredient from your selected recipes and QuickBites appears on the
list, grouped by aisle. Quantities from multiple recipes are combined — if
two recipes call for onions, you see one entry with the total amount.

## Live updates

The list is shared. If another household member checks a recipe or marks an
item, you'll see the change immediately without refreshing.

## Screen stays on

The app keeps your screen from dimming on the Groceries page, so you
don't have to keep tapping while you're shopping. The same applies when
[viewing a recipe]({{ site.baseurl }}/recipes/cooking/) while cooking.

## Building the list

Select recipes and QuickBites on the [Menu]({{ site.baseurl }}/menu/) page.
Their ingredients appear here automatically. Add anything that's not in a
recipe using [Custom items]({{ site.baseurl }}/groceries/custom-items/).
